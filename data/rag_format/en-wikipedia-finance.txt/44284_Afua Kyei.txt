# Afua Kyei



**Afua Kyei** is a British financial executive and has been the Chief Financial Officer (CFO) of the Bank of England since 2019. She was formerly the Finance Director at Barclays Bank LLC. In January 2023, Kyei was listed among the 100 most reputable Africans. Kyei was included as one of the most influential black people in the UK on the 2024 *Powerlist*.

In 2023, she was elected as Honorary Fellow of Somerville College, Oxford.


## Education

Kyei earned her bachelor's and master's degrees from Somerville College, Oxford. She then became a junior researcher in organic chemistry at Princeton University.

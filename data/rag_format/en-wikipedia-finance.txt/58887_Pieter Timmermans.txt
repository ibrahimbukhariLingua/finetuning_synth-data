# Pieter Timmermans



**Pieter, Baron Timmermans** (born 14 June 1964 in Ninove) is a Belgian businessman and the CEO of the Federation of Belgian Enterprises.


## Education

He obtained a master's degree in business economics from the KU Leuven.


## Career

Since May 2012, Pieter Timmermans has been the CEO of the Federation of Belgian Enterprises, and since 27 May 2013, he has served as a regent of the National Bank of Belgium.

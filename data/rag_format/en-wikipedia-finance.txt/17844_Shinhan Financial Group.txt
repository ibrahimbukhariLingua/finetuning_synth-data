# Shinhan Financial Group



**Shinhan Financial Group Co., Ltd.** (Korean: 주식회사 신한금융지주회사; RR: *Jusik Hoesa Sinhan Geumyung Jiju Hoesa*) is a financial holding company headquartered in Seoul, South Korea. Its subsidiaries provide a full range of financial services, including banking, securities, life insurance, and investment banking. It is one of Korea's so-called Big Five financial groups, along with KB Financial Group, NH Financial Group, Hana Financial Group, and Woori Financial Group.

# Iraimbilanja



The **iraimbilanja** (singular and plural) is the divisory currency unit of Madagascar, being equal to one fifth of an ariary.


## Etymology

Iraimbilanja means literally "one iron weight" and was the name of an old coin worth 1⁄5 of an ariary.


## Value

The old Malagasy franc is equal in value to one iraimbilanja. As of 1 January 2023 the value of one iraimbilanja is US$0.000044 /¢0.0044. ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ

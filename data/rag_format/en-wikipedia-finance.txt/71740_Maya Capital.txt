# Maya Capital



**Maya Capital** is a British specialist real estate and private equity investment firm based in London. The firm's major activities to date have concentrated on investment in UK real estate outside London. Their strategy focuses on creating initiatives, such as asset repositioning, financial structuring, property development, and seeking opportunities resulting from special situations.

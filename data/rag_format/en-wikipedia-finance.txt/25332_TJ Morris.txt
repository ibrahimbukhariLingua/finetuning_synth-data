# TJ Morris



**T J Morris Group Limited** is a British company that owns several businesses, including the discount store chain Home Bargains.


## Home Bargains

The main business of TJ Morris is the chain Home Bargains, which had 595 stores in the UK as of January 2025. The current slogan for Home Bargains is *Top Brands, Bottom Prices*.


## Quality Save (2023-2024)

TJ Morris was the main owner of the discount chain Quality Save when they acquired it in January 2023, they rebranded their stores to Home Bargains, with it finishing on 1 April 2024.

# Luoyang Glass



**Luoyang Glass Company Limited** or **Luoyang Glass** (SEHK:Â 1108, SSE: 600876) is a state-owned enterprise in Luoyang, Henan, China, which is involved with the production and sales of float sheet and flat glass and reprocessing of automobile glass.


## History

Luoyang Glass was established in 1994 by its parent company, **China Luoyang Float Glass Group**. Its H shares were listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange in 1994, while its A shares were listed on the Shanghai Stock Exchange in 1995.

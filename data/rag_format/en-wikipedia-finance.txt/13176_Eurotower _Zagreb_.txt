# Eurotower (Zagreb)



**Eurotower** is a high-rise building in Zagreb, Croatia, located in Trnje at the intersection of the Vukovarska and Lučićeva streets, in the southwest corner. It was built in 2006. This office tower has 26 levels and it is 96 m (315 ft) tall. The Embassy of the Republic of Lithuania to the Republic of Croatia, Montenegro, the Republic of Kosovo, and the Republic of North Macedonia is located in the building.

Eurotower is currently best known as the headquarters of the Zagreb Stock Exchange and KPMG in Croatia.
The architect is Marijan Hržić, who is also the designer of the Cibona Tower in Zagreb.

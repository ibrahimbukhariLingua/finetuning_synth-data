# Adolf Weber (economist)



**Adolf Weber** (29 December 1876 – 5 January 1963) was a German economist.


## Life

Adolf Weber was born at Mechernich in the Rhine Province near Bonn. He was professor at the University of Cologne, the University of Breslau, the University of Frankfurt and the University of Munich. Weber was a friend of Ludwig von Mises, and broadly associated with the Austrian School. He criticised the economic policy of Reichsbank president Hjalmar Schacht in an article 'Is Schacht right?'

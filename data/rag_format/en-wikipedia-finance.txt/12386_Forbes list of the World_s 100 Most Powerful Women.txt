# Forbes list of the World's 100 Most Powerful Women



Since 2004, *Forbes*, an American business magazine, has published an annual list of its ranking of the 100 most powerful women in the world. Edited by prominent *Forbes* journalists, including Moira Forbes, the list is compiled using various criteria such as visibility and economic impact. In 2024, the gauge was "money, media, impact and spheres of influence". The top 10 per year are listed below.

1. Ursula von der Leyen, President of the European Commission
2. Christine Lagarde, President of the European Central Bank
3. Giorgia Meloni, Prime Minister of Italy

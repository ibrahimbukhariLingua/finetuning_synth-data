# European Smaller Companies Trust



**European Smaller Companies Trust** (LSE:Â ESCT), formerly **TR European Growth Trust**, is a large British investment trust focused on investments in smaller European companies. Established in 1990, the company is a constituent of the FTSE 250 Index, an index of the larger companies on the London Stock Exchange. The company is managed by Janus Henderson and the chairman is Christopher Casey.


## History

The company was established under the management of Henderson Group in 1990. It changed its name from TR European Growth Trust to European Smaller Companies Trust in October 2021.

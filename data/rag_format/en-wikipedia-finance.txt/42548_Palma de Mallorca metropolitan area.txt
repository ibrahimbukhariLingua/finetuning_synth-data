# Palma de Mallorca metropolitan area



The **Palma de Mallorca metropolitan area** (Catalan: *Àrea metropolitana de Palma*, Spanish: *Área metropolitana de Palma de Mallorca*) is the metropolitan area of Palma de Mallorca. The metropolitan area is located in the Mallorca island with an area of 979 km²


## Economy

In 2020 Palma de Mallorca gross metropolitan product was €21.456 billion. This puts Palma de Mallorca in 108th place among cities in European Union.

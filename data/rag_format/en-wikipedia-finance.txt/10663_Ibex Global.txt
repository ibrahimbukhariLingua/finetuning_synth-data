# Ibex Global



**Ibex Global**, formerly known as **TRG Customer Solutions**, is an American business process outsourcing company based in Washington, D.C..


## History

Ibex was founded in 2002 as TRG Customer Solutions. It is partially owned by TRG Pakistan.

In 2014, Ibex opened a call center in Spring Hill, Tennessee.

In 2018, Ibex closed its call center in Indiana and laid off 105 employees.

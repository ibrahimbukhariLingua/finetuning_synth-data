# Salman Shah (economist)



**Salman Shah** (Urdu: سلمان شاہ) is a Pakistani economist who served as an Advisor to the Chief Minister of Punjab, Usman Buzdar, on Economic Affairs and Planning & Development. Previously, he has served as the caretaker Finance Minister of Pakistan. He has served as an advisor to Finance Minister Shaukat Aziz on finance, economic affairs, statistics and revenues.


## Personal life

He is the son-in-law of former Chief of Army Staff General Asif Nawaz Janjua. He has two sons and three daughters, including Khadijah Shah.

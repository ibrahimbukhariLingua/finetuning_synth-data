# Servcorp



**Servcorp Limited** is a multinational organisation that sells Serviced Offices, Virtual Offices, Coworking Spaces, Meeting Rooms, Community Packages, and IT Services to entrepreneurs, startups, SME's and large enterprises.

It was established in 1978 and listed on the Australian Stock Exchange in 1999. As of 9 December 2020, it operates in 125+ business centres in 43 cities across 21 countries. The company currently has 85 coworking locations globally.

Servcorp has registered and owns a trademark for the phrase "Everything but the office".


## History

Servcorp was founded by Alfred Moufarrige in 1978. In 1980 Servcorp pioneered the virtual office concept. In 1990, Servcorp expanded to Singapore, then throughout South East Asia, France and Japan. Servcorp was listed on the Australian Stock Exchange in 1999.

In 1999, Servcorp launched its first Chinese offices in Shanghai. In 2003, Servcorp was the leading Australian employer in Japan with 130 Tokyo staff. Servcorp opened its first United Kingdom office in 2009. It opened its first United States location in 2010. Servcorp opened its first office in İstanbul Turkey in 2010 and second one in 2011 in Tekfen Tower.

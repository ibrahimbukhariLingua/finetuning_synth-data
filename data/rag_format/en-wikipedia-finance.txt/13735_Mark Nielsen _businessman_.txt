# Mark Nielsen (businessman)



**Mark Nielsen** (born September 27, 1968) is an Australian business executive and CEO of Talent, a minor recruitment company in Australia in the technology sector.


## Early life and career

Mark Nielsen was born in Johannesburg, South Africa and completed a Bachelor of Commerce degree at the University of Cape Town. In 1994, he qualified as a chartered accountant. In 2002, he moved to Australia. He is a board member and co-founder of Talent's foundation, Talent RISE, which has helped over 50 disadvantaged youth access employment. Talent RISE has since ceased operations under Nielsen's tenure.

# Crown (currency)



A **crown** is a unit of currency used in Norway, Sweden, Denmark (including the Faroe Islands and Greenland), Iceland, and the Czech Republic.


## Alternative names

"Crown", or its equivalent in other languages, is derived from the Latin word *corona*. The symbol for crown is usually "kr". Some countries use another symbol for it like Íkr, -, Kč.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ

The local name for "crown" depends on the official language of the country.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ


### Current use

* Czech: koruna
* Norwegian and Danish: krone
* Icelandic and Faroese: króna
* Swedish: krona
* Northern Sami: ruvdna

### Historical use

* Estonian: kroon
* German: Krone (capital letter k)
* Hungarian: korona
* Slovak: koruna

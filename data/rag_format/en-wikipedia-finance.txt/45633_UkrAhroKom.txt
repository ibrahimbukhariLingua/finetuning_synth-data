# UkrAhroKom



**UkrAhroKom** (Ukrainian: УкрАгроКом) together with "Hermes-Treidynh" (Ukrainian: Гермес-Трейдинг) is a Ukrainian group of agriculture companies. It is headquartered in Oleksandriia Raion, Kirovohrad Oblast. In July 2018, the company was rebranded as AhroVista (Russian: АгроВиста).

The company is one of the largest agricultural companies in Ukraine.


## History

The company was founded in the village of Holovkivka. From 2008 to 2014, the holding owned the Football Club UkrAhroKom, and merged with FC Oleksandriya in 2014.

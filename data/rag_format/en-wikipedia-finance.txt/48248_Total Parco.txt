# Total Parco



**Total Parco Pakistan Limited**, stylized as **TOTAL PARCO**, is a Pakistani oil marketing company which is a subsidiary of French company Total S.A. It is based in Lahore, Pakistan.

Total Parco the second largest petrol pump network in Pakistan. It operates more than 800 petrol pumps.


## History

It was founded in 2002 as a joint venture between Pak-Arab Refinery (PARCO) and Total S.A.

In 2013, Total announced a plan to acquire 438 petrol stations of Chevron Corporation Pakistan. The acquisition was complated in 2015 and Caltex petrol pumps were renamed as Parco Total.

In 2024, Gunvor acquired 50 percent stake of TotalEnergies in Total Parco.

# Centric Software



**Centric Software** is a Silicon Valley–based software company headquartered in Campbell, California. The company provides Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) software, as well as planning, pricing and market Intelligence solutionsᵇᵘᶻᶻʷᵒʳᵈ, for consumer goods such as fashion, multi-category retail, outdoor, luxury, grocery including formulated products (Food & Beverage and Cosmetic & Personal Care) and consumer electronics.


## History

Centric Software was founded in 1998. The company began as a product lifecycle management vendor and then made developments in enterprise mobility applications suite with visual, touch-screen based digital board solutions.ᵇᵘᶻᶻʷᵒʳᵈ

The company has provided product lifecycle management to retailers, brands and manufacturers including Volcom, Tesco, Louis Vuitton, SIPLEC, Balenciaga, and Calvin Klein among others.

In 2006, Centric Software Launched Centric PLM, the first out-of-the-box PLM solutionᵇᵘᶻᶻʷᵒʳᵈ specifically for the fashion industry.

In 2010, Centric introduced its first mobile application, Capture it, which uploads images to the PLM system.

In 2018, Centric began to add 3D connectors that integrate Centric PLM with applications including Browzwear, Optitex and CLO 3D.

In 2019, Centric launched PLM for OEM and ODM manufacturers.

In 2020, Centric enters into new verticals, expanding into Food & Beverage and Cosmetics & Personal Care.

Centric Software acquired retail planning provider, Armonica Retail in 2021. This was followed by the acquisition of market intelligence software company StyleSgae in 2022 and the predictive pricing software provider aifora, in 2023.

As of May 2024, the company has 19 offices around the world and 6 virtual centers. Its investors include Dassault Systèmes (3DS), and employees.


## Products

Centric Software has integrated 3D, mobility, AI, cloud, SaaS, and Agile Development to develop their products.

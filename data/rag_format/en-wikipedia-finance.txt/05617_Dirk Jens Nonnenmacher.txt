# Dirk Jens Nonnenmacher



**Dirk Jens Nonnenmacher** (born 3 June 1963) was the CEO and chairman of HSH Nordbank. A trained mathematician, Nonnenmacher worked for several private banks before joining HSH Nordbank in 2007. He became chairman in November 2008, succeeding Hans Berger [de] and left the bank in March 2011.


## Biography

Nonnenmacher studied mathematics and medicine, before receiving his Ph.D. under supervision of Wolfgang B. Jurkat [de] in 1990. He earned his habilitation in 1993.

# Capital Department, Catamarca



**Capital** is a department of Catamarca Province in Argentina.

The provincial subdivision has a population of 159,703 inhabitants in an area of 684 km² (264 sq mi), and its capital city is San Fernando del Valle de Catamarca, which is located around 1,145 km (711 mi) from Buenos Aires.

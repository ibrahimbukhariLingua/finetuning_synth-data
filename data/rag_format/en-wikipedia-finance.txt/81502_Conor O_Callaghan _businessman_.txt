# Conor O'Callaghan (businessman)






**Conor O'Callaghan** is an American politician and business executive. He previously worked for Wall Street companies like Nomura Securities and other companies on Wall Street. He is a candidate for Arizona's 1st Congressional District as a Democrat.

O'Callaghan was born in Ireland and immigrated to Arizona with his family at a young age. He received degrees from University of Pennsylvania. He was an unindicted co-conspirator in a case against his former employer, Nomura.


## Congressional candidacy

O'Callaghan announced his intention to run for U.S. Congress in Arizona's 1st congressional district in the 2024 elections on August 1, 2023.

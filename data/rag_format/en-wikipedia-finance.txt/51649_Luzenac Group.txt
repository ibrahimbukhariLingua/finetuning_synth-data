# Luzenac Group



The **Luzenac Group** is a talc mining company headquartered in Toulouse, France. The company is the world's largest producer of talc,ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ with mines and processing plants located in Europe, North America, Australia, and East Asia. The Luzenac Group was a wholly owned subsidiary of the Rio Tinto Group from 1988
until August 2011, when it was sold to Imerys.

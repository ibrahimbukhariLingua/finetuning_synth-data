# Aju Business Daily



**Aju News Corporation** (Korean: 아주경제; Hanja: 亞洲經濟; also known as **Aju News**) is a economic newspaper launched in October 2007 by former *Korea Economic Daily* and *Seoul Economic Daily [ko]* reporter Kwak Young-gil, and published in South Korea. Based in Seoul, it is a major newspaper in South Korea.


## History

*Aju Business Daily* was founded in October 2007. In October 2010, it began to cooperate with Hong Kong–based *Wen Wei Po* to provide news about the Chinese economy.

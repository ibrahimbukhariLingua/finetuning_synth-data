# Pittsburgh Business Times



The ***Pittsburgh Business Times*** is a diversified business media company serving Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. It publishes daily stories on its website and social networks, and a weekly edition available in print and online. It is published by the American City Business Journals.


## History

Published since August 31, 1981, the newspaper was founded by the Business Journal Publications Company which merged the paper with the rival *Pittsburgh Business Journal*, owned by Scripps-Howard, on March 21, 1985. They combined operations by April 1 of that year. In 1986, Business Journal Publications was acquired by American City Business Journals. The paper was sold in 1988. American City Business Journals reacquired the *Business Times* in 1996 with purchase of CityMedia Inc.

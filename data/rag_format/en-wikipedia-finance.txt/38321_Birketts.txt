# Birketts



**Birketts LLP** is one of the oldest law firms based in the UK. It was established in 1863 by Benjamin Birkett. The firm is often cited by the mainstream media for opinions and analysis on notable cases.


## Overview

Birketts is a full-service law firm headquartered in Ipswich with additional offices in Cambridge, Chelmsford, London, Norwich and Sevenoaks. Their business is divided into four main disciplines which are corporate and commercial, commercial property, commercial and personal litigation, and private client.

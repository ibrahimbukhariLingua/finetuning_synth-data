# Vietnam International Commercial Joint Stock Bank



**The Vietnam International Commercial Joint Stock Bank** *(Vietnamese: *Ngân hàng TMCP Quốc tế Việt Nam*),* abbreviated as "**VIB**", is a commercial bank in Vietnam. It was established in 1996 and is headquartered in Ho Chi Minh City. VIB is one of the largest banks in Vietnam, with a total asset of over VND 383,000 billion (US$16.5 billion) as of December 30, 2023.

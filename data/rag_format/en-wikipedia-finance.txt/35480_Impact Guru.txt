# Impact Guru



**Impact Guru** is a donation based crowdfunding platform for NGOs, social enterprises, startups and individuals. It has raised ₹150 crore (US$18 million) for various NGOs and social enterprises in more than 15 countries.

Impact Guru was incubated at Harvard Innovation Lab’s Venture Initiation Program in 2014 and PACT at Impact Hub in Singapore in 2015. Impact Guru was founded by Piyush Jain and Khushboo Jain. It was launched by Maneka Gandhi in September 2015.


## Funding

In April 2015, Impact Guru raised ₹3.3 crore (US$400,000) seed money from RB Investments, a Singapore-based venture capital firm, and Fundnel, a private investment platform based in Southeast Asia.


## Partnerships

In April 2016, Impact Guru partnered with Singaporean investment platform Fundnel to gather investment capital from the Indian diaspora in Southeast Asia.

In September 2016, Impact Guru partnered with United Nations entity named UN Women to run *Project Inspire*, an initiative by Singapore Committee for UN Women, in partnership with Bain & Company and MasterCard, to support social entrepreneurs working towards women's empowerment.

In October 2016, Impact Guru partnered with US and UK based crowdfunding platform, GlobalGiving.

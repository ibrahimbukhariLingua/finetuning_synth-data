# Privatization Board of Turkey



The **Privatization Administration of Turkey** (Turkish: *T.C. Özelleştirme İdaresi Başkanlığı*), founded on 27 November 1994, Law No.4046, is a government agency in Turkey. It is responsible for the privatization process of public sector companies as well as other public assets and services.

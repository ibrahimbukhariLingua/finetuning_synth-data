# Master Trader



***Master Trader*** is a video game published by Argon Games.


## Development

*Master Trader* is a version of a game that originally appeared in the book *What to Do After You Hit Return*.


## Gameplay

*Master Trader* is a game in which 2 to 12 players buy and sell products throughout the universe for profit.


## Reception

Ian Chadwick reviewed *Master Trader* in *Ares Magazine* #12 and commented that "*Trader* is not a bad game, just not terribly exciting. Broderbund's *Galactic Trader* may be a better game (especially visually) but it is only solitaire play."

# Bev (company)



**Bev** is a canned wine supplier based in Venice, California. It is owned by E. & J. Gallo Winery.


## History

Bev was founded in 2017 by Alix Peabody. The wine was initially marketed online. The company eventually signed a distribution deal with wine and spirits distributor Southern Glazer's Wine and Spirits.

In February 2021, the company signed a distribution deal with E & J Gallo Winery to distribute Bev's wines nationally. The deal would allow the wines to be sold in retailers including Target and Bevmo.

The company was acquired by E. & J. Gallo Winery in 2023.

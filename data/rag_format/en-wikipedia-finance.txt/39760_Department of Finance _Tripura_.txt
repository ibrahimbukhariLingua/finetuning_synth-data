# Department of Finance (Tripura)



The **Department of Finance** is one of the departments of Government of Tripura. It is responsible for managing the public finances and presenting the budget of the state government.


## Objective

The department is responsible for manage state finances, including budgeting, expenditure, revenue generation, and fiscal policies, to ensure sustainable economic growth and financial stability.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ


## Sub departments

* Directorate of Treasuries
* Taxes and Excise Organisation
* Audit Directorate
* Internal Audit Wing
* Local Fund Audit Wing

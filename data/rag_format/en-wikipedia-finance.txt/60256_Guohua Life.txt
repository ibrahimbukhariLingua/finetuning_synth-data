# Guohua Life



**Guohua Life Insurance Co., Ltd.** is a Chinese life insurance company headquartered in Shanghai. Since 2016, the company was a subsidiary (51%) of Biocause Pharmaceutical (SZSE: 000627), after the acquisition of the shares was approved by the China Insurance Regulatory Commission in the same year. The deal was announced in 2015.


## Equity investments

* HNA Technology (14.33% as insurance-linked equity fund manager)

## Sponsorship

Guohua Life was the naming rights sponsor of a Shanghai-based women volleyball team from 2011 to circa 2013.

# JPMorgan Asia Growth & Income



**JPMorgan Asia Growth & Income** (LSE:Â JAGI) is a large British investment trust dedicated to investments in Asia excluding Japan. The company is listed on the London Stock Exchange. The Chairman is Bronwyn Curtis.


## History

The company was established in 1997. It changed its name from JPMorgan Asian Investment Trust to JPMorgan Asia Growth & Income on 13 February 2020.

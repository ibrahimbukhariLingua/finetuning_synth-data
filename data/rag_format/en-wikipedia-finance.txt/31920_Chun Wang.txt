# Chun Wang



**Chun Wang** (Chinese: 王纯; pinyin: *Wáng Chún*ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ) is a Chinese-born Maltese cryptocurrency investor and entrepreneur.


## Biography

Wang was born in Tianjin, China. He later moved to Thailand in 2015, then to South Korea. He is now a citizen of Malta.


## Bitcoin mining

In 2013, Wang co-founded F2Pool, one of the first Bitcoin mining pools in China and one of the largest globally.


## Private spaceflight

In August 2024, SpaceX announced that Wang would be funding and commanding the Fram2 mission of a Crew Dragon spacecraft, which aims to be the first human flight to go over the Earth's poles.

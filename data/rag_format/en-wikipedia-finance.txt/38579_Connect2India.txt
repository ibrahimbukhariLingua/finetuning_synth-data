# Connect2India



**Connect2India** is an international trading platform that enables micro, small and medium enterprises (SMEs) to import from and export to India.


## History


### Foundation

The company was founded December of 2015 by Pawan Gupta. He developed the platform to present transparent, accurate, analytical and complete information on trading parameters, which Indian SME traders previously lacked.


### Expansion

Connect2India uses blockchain technology combined with GPS, RFID and IoT to counter International Trade Frauds. It is a platform where an enterprise or an individual from anywhere can find global opportunities to trade from India. It was later initiated in both tier 2 and tier 3 cities of India.


## Business

Connect2India is world's trade information provider. Its service delivers to twenty cities of India that includes Pune, Ahmedabad, Ludhiana, Indore, Kanpur, Hyderabad, Bengaluru, Delhi Surat, Jaipur, Rajkot and Varanasi. Enterprises from countries can draw trade information and get trading solutions, including countries like Germany, France, Belgium, Italy and Spain. Support sectors like agriculture, machinery, handicrafts, paper, garments, etc.

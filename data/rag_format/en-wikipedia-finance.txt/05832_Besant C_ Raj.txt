# Besant C. Raj



**Besant C. Raj** (5 February 1933 â€“ 12 March 2018) was an Indian management and financial consultant. He was a founding member of ICFAI University. He was an advisor to many government and non-government organizations in the state of Chennai, Mumbai, Kerala and Andhra Pradesh, India. He was closely associated with several educational institutions like the Jawaharlal Nehru Institute of Development Banking of IDBI and was the chancellor of the ICFAI University, Dehradun, Uttaranchal.


## Education

He holds a doctoral degree in business administration, specializing in financial management from the Harvard Business School. He was an MBA from the Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad (first batch) and had a master's degree in philosophy from Madras University and a master's degree in psychology from Banaras Hindu University.

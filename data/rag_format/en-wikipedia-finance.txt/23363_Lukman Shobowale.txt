# Lukman Shobowale



**Lukman Olawale Shobowale** (born 29 May 1994) is a Nigerian writer and businessman. He is the co-founder and CEO Dukiya Investments.


## Biography

Lukman is the founder of Dukiya Investments Limited, a Lagos-based real estate development and construction firm. He is an author and also serves as the head of Business Development at Dukiya Investments LTD. Lukman is known for real estate, business, writing, and leadership. He advocates for the use of real estate to build wealth for future generations. During his undergraduate degree in History and International Studies at the University of Ilorin, he served as the President of the Student Union for the 2016/2017 academic year. Lukman won the 2023 Entrepreneur Awards in 2023 by The Future Awards Africa. Lukman is a volunteer who also engages in philanthropic activities.


## Education

Lukman is an alumnus of the University of Ilorin, where he studied History and International Studies for his Bachelor of Art Degree before proceeding to Ajayi Crowther University for a Masters in Business Administration in 2021. He also went further to the Lagos Business School for Owner Managers Programme. He had an executive programs at Rome Business School in 2023 and London Business School in 2024.

# Ethiopian Insurance Corporation



The **Ethiopian Insurance Corporation** (Amharic: የኢትዮጵያ ኢንሹራንስ ኮርፖሬሽን; **EIC**) is a state-owned insurance corporation headquartered in Leghar, Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. It was established in 1976 with initial capital of 11 million (1.29 million USD) after taking over the thirteen nationalized private insurance companies liabilities and assets.


## History

The Ethiopian Insurance Corporation (EIC) was established in 1 January 1976 by proclamation No. 68/1975 by emerging takeover of the thirteen nationalized private insurance companies liabilities and assets, with birr 11 million (USD 1.29 million) paid up capital.

It is state-owned and profitable organization. As of August 2023, the Corporation profits plunged up to 1.4 trillion birr.

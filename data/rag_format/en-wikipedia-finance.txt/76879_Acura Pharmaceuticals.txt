# Acura Pharmaceuticals



**Acura Pharmaceuticals Inc.** is a pharmaceutical company focused on the development and commercialization of deterrents to medication abuse and misuse. As of 2012ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, the company had several opioid products under development, which would use "Aversion Technology". As of 2014ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, it was a publicly traded company, listed on NASDAQ under the symbol "ACUR".


## History

In 2013, the Company settled Oxecta patent litigation with Impax Laboratories (IPXL) and Par Pharmaceutical.

**License agreements**

The company has an agreement to license, develop and commercialize opioid analgesic products with King Pharmaceuticals.

**Product adoptions**

In 2013, multiple retailers – including Kroger and Fruth Pharmacy – stocked Acura's product Nexafed.

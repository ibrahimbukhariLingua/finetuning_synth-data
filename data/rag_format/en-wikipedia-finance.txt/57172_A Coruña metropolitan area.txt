# A Coruña metropolitan area



The **A Coruña metropolitan area** (known in Spanish as: *Área metropolitana de La Coruña*) is the metropolitan area of A Coruña. The metropolitan area is located in the Province of A Coruña, with an area of 1,748 km²


## Economy

In 2020 the A Coruña gross metropolitan product was €25.231 billion. This puts A Coruña in the 91st place among cities in the European Union.

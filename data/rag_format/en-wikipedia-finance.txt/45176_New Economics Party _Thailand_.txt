# New Economics Party (Thailand)



The **New Economics Party** (NEP) (Thai: พรรคเศรษฐกิจใหม่) is a political party in Thailand founded in March 2018 by Supadit Arkartruek.


## Election results

| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 2019 | 6 / 500 | 485,664 | 1.34 | 6 seats; **5 Junior partner in governing coalition, 1 Opposition** | Mingkwan Saengsuwan |

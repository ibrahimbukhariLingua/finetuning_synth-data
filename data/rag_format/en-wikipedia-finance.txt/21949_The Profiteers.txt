# The Profiteers



***The Profiteers*** is a 1919 silent film drama directed by George Fitzmaurice and starring Fannie Ward. It was produced by Astra Film Corp. and released through Path√© Exchange.

## Preservation

With no prints of *The Profiteers* located in any film archives, it is considered a lost film. In February of 2021, the film was cited by the National Film Preservation Board on their Lost U.S. Silent Feature Films list.

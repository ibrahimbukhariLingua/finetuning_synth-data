# Ministry of Finance (Botswana)



The **Ministry of Finance** is the Botswana government ministry which formulates financial and economic policies and oversees effective coordination of Government financial operations. As of April 2021ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, the minister is Peggy Serame.

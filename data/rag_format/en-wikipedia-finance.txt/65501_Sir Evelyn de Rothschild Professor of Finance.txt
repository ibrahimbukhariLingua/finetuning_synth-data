# Sir Evelyn de Rothschild Professor of Finance



The **Sir Evelyn de Rothschild Professorship of Finance** is an established chair at the University of Cambridge. The chair is currently assigned to Judge Business School which is the Faculty of Business and Management at Cambridge University.

# Plug-in electric vehicles in Brazil



As of 2021ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, 7% of new cars sold in Brazil were electric.


## Government policy

As of October 2022ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, the Brazilian government does not charge any import taxes on electric vehicles.


## Charging stations

As of 2022ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, there were 1,300 public charging stations in Brazil.


## Public opinion

In a 2022 survey conducted by Tupinambá Energia, 58% of prospective car buyers were interested in buying an electric vehicle.


## By state


### Goiás

As of November 2022ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, there were 27 public charging stations in Goiás.

As of November 2022ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, there were 19 public charging stations in Goiânia.


### Rio de Janeiro

As of November 2022ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, there were about 3,000 electric vehicles in the state of Rio de Janeiro.

As of November 2022ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, there were about 2,000 electric vehicles in the city of Rio de Janeiro.


### São Paulo

As of August 2022ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, there were 445 public charging stations in the city of São Paulo.

# Vasanth & Co



**Vasanth & Co** is a chain dealer of consumer electronics and home appliances in Tamil Nadu, India. The company was founded in 1978 and As of 2020ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ has 84 showrooms. The company was founded and owned by the late H. Vasanthakumar.


## Expansion

The chain reported a turnover of ₹ 5 billion (100 million US dollars) in the year 2007, which had doubled by 2020.


## SAP implementation

The company chose to implement the mySAP ERP in 50 of its showrooms.


## Founder

The founder H. Vasanthakumar was born in 1950. He died on 28 August 2020, due to coronavirus.

# Korea Sogyong Trading



The **Korea Sogyong Trading Corporation** is a North Korean company that exports carpets and manufactures cigarettes.


## Origins

Its cigarette manufacturing business began in September 2001 when it formed a 40%-60% joint venture with British American Tobacco called Taesong-BAT. As of 2005ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, when the venture became known to the public, Taesong-BAT employed 200 people in Pyongyang and produced 2 billion cigarettes per year.

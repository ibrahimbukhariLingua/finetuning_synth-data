# Heungkuk Life



**Heungkuk Life Insurance Co, Ltd.** (Korean: 흥국생명; established 1950) is a South Korean insurance company headquartered in Seoul, South Korea.


## Information

It is a part of Taekwang Group, Korea's 40th largest chaebol. It is an insurance company that deals with life, pensions, and children's insurance.

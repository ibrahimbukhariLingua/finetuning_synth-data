# IJEX



**IJEX Exchange Co., Ltd.** , branded **IJEX**, is a US-based multi-asset investment company focused on CFD offerings, and has offices in Singapore, Canada, and the United Kingdom.


## Overview

IJEX is headquartered in the United States and has offices in Singapore, Canada, and the United Kingdom. IJEX is regulated by the Accounting and Corporate Regulatory Authority (ACRA), a ministry of the government of Singapore responsible for managing Singapore's fiscal policies and the structure of its economy.

The **Ministry of Industrialisation and Trade** (**MIT**) is a government ministry of Namibia, with headquarters in Windhoek. It was created at Namibian independence in 1990 as **Ministry of Trade and Industry**, renamed **Ministry of Industrialisation, Trade and SME Development** in 2015, and got its current name in 2020. The first Namibian minister of trade was Ben Amathila. The currentᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ minister is Lucia Iipumbu.

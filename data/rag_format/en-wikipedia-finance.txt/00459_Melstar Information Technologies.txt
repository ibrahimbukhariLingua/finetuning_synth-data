# Melstar Information Technologies




**Melstar Information Technologies** (BSE: 532307) is a professional service and workforce solutionᵇᵘᶻᶻʷᵒʳᵈ provider based in Mumbai, Maharashtra. Is having branch offices at Gurgaon, Chennai, Bangalore. Its main domains includes Banking, Insurance, Government and IT.


## History

It was established in 1986, Mumbai. Till 1998, Melstar has been mainly engaged in trading of hardware. The company shifted its focus to software development in 1998. It was listed on BSE in 2000.

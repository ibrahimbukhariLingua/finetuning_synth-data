# State Anti-Monopoly Bureau



The **State Anti-Monopoly Bureau** (Chinese: 国家反垄断局) is an administrative agency under the State Council governing antitrust. It is an external name of the State Administration for Market Regulation.


## History

On 15 November 2021, Gan Lin was appointed director of the State Anti-Monopoly Bureau, which was formally established on November 18.

# UPTX



**Up Global Holding Company Ltd.**, branded **UPTX**, is a Cayman Islands financial company. UPTX focuses on asset management and focuses on stocks, foreign exchange, commodities.


## Overview

UPTX is founded by Kelly Xu(Chinese:徐可; pinyin:Xu Ke),who founded the China's First Blockchain Social Network. It currently has offices in over 130 countries and regions, including Singapore, Dubai, Kuala Lumpur, Tokyo, Hong Kong, London.

UPTX is regulated by the U.S. MSB(Money Services Business), NFA, and Lithuania's VASP, a federal body overseeing the securities industry, including investment advisory firms.

On October 17, 2024, UP GLOBAL signed a cooperation agreement with Huawei to provide technical support for UPTX.

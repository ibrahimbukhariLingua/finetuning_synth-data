# Tata Capital



**Tata Capital Limited** is a financial and investment service provider in India. The company is based in Mumbai and has more than 700 branches across the country. The firm offers consumer loans, wealth management, commercial finance, and infrastructure finance, among others.


## Overview

Tata Capital, a subsidiary of Tata Sons Limited, was established in the year 2007. It is the financial services arm of the Tata Group.

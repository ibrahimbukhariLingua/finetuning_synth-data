# Robinhood, Mississippi



**Robinhood** is an unincorporated community and census-designated place in Rankin County, Mississippi, United States. Per the 2020 census, the population was 1,491.


## Geography

According to the U.S. Census Bureau, the community has an area of 5.266 square miles (13.64 km²); 5.080 square miles (13.16 km²) of its area is land, and 0.186 square miles (0.48 km²) is water.

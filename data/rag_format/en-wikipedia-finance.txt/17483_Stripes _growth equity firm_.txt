# Stripes (growth equity firm)



**Stripes** (Stripes, LLC) is a private equity and venture capital firm based in Manhattan, New York, founded in 2008. In 2024, Stripes has US$7 billion of assets under management and more than 80 investments. Notable investments include On Running and Monday.com.


## History

Stripes was founded by Ken Fox in 2008.

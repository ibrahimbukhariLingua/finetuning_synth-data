# Voss Veksel- og Landmandsbank



**Voss Veksel- og Landmandsbank** (OSE: VVL) or **Vekselbanken** is a Norwegian commercial bank located in Voss, Hordaland. It is the only remaining independent commercial bank that had not merged into DnB NOR, Nordea, Fokus Bank or a sparebank.


## History

The bank is listed on the Oslo Stock Exchange and was founded in 1899 to provide services to the labourers working on building the Bergen Line through Voss.

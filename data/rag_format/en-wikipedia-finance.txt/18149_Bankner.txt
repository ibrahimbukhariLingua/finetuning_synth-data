# Bankner



**Bankner** is a census town in North West district in the state of Delhi, India.

As of 2001ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ India census, Bankner had a population of 21,085. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Bankner has an average literacy rate of 80%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with 85% of the males and 75% of females literate. 16% of the population is under six years of age.

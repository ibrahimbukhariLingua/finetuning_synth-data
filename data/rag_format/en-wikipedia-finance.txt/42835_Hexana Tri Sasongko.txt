# Hexana Tri Sasongko



**Hexana Tri Sasongko** (born March 23, 1964) is an Indonesian banker. On March 21, 2023, he was appointed as the chief executive officer of state owned insurance company *Indonesia Financial Group*.

He was involved in efforts to rescue life insurance policyholders at Jiwasraya through a restructuring mechanism during the Jiwasraya scandal.


## Education

Hexana Tri Sasongko was born in Klaten, Central Java, on March 23, 1964. He obtained his Bachelor of Law degree from Airlangga University in 1986, and later earned a Master of Business Administration degree from Monash University, Melbourne, Australia, in 1996.

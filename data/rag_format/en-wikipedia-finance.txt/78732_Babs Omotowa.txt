# Babs Omotowa



**Babs Omotowa** was the former managing director of Nigeria Liquefied Natural Gas Limited (NLNG) and vice president of the Shell Global, and served on the board of the Nigerian Economic Summit Group.


## Biography and education

Omotowa was born in Kogi state, Nigeria. He holds a bachelor's degree in Industrial Chemistry, University of Ilorin, and an MBA, University of Leicester, UK. In 2011, Omotowa was appointed as the CEO and managing director of Nigeria LNG, the country's leading liquefied natural gas company. Omotowa previously served on the board of the Nigerian Economic Summit Group.

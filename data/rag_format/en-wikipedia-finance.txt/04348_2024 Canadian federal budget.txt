# 2024 Canadian federal budget



The **Canadian federal budget** for the fiscal years of 2024–25 was presented to the House of Commons by Finance Minister Chrystia Freeland on 16 April 2024. The budget's slogan is "Fairness for every generation", suggesting the government planned to help younger people.


## Background

In March 2022, the New Democratic Party agreed to a confidence and supply deal with Justin Trudeau's Liberal Party.


## Measures

The budget's main goal is to reduce the cost of living.

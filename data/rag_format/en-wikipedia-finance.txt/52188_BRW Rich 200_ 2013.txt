# BRW Rich 200, 2013



The ***BRW* Rich 200, 2013** is the 30th annual survey of the wealthiest people resident in Australia, published in hardcopy and online formats in the *BRW* magazine on 24 May 2013. This was the last list published in hardcopy format by the *BRW* as the final hardcopy issue of the *BRW* was published in November 2013.

In the 2013 list, the net worth of the wealthiest individual, Gina Rinehart, was A$22.02 billion.

The *BRW Rich Families List* was published annually since 2008. In the 2013 list, the Smorgon family headed the list with estimated wealth of A$2.64 billion. The Smorgon families headed the families list in every year of its publication. The families list was last published in 2015.

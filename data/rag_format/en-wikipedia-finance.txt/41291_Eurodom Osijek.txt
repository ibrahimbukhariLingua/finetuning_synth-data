# Eurodom Osijek



**Eurodom** is a business, shopping and cultural center in Osijek, Croatia.

It is named after Eurodom company, which was established in 2002.

The whole complex is 85,000 m² (910,000 sq ft) and it consists of three buildings, two are business towers and the third is a shopping mall and cultural center.


## Business tower

Business tower consists of two towers, which are popularly called *Osijek Twins* (Croatian: *Osječki blizanci*). It has twelve floors and the height is 61 m (200 ft).

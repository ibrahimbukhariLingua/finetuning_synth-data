# Daniel Scott Johnson



**Daniel Scott Johnson** (born February 24, 1979) is an American financial advisor, investment advisor, and the founder of Windfall Advisors.


## Career

In 2008, Daniel Johnson took on the role of vice president financial advisor at Merrill Lynch. By 2020, he established Windfall Advisors, a fee-only fiduciary and registered investment advisory firm.

Johnson served on the board of directors for both United Cerebral Palsy Wheels of LA Division and the Malibu Chamber of commerce.

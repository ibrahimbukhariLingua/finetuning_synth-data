# Communauté d'agglomération Grand Montauban



**Communauté d'agglomération Grand Montauban** is the *communauté d'agglomération*, an intercommunal structure, centred on the city of Montauban. It is located in the Tarn-et-Garonne department, in the Occitania region, southern France. Created in 1999, its seat is in Montauban. Its area is 280.9 km². Its population was 78,505 in 2019, of which 61,372 in Montauban proper.

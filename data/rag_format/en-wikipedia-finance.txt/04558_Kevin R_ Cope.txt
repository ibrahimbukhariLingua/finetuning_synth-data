# Kevin R. Cope



***Kevin R. Cope*** (born January 26, 1962) is an author and co-founder and CEO of a company called Acumen Learning.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ He is the author of Seeing the Big Picture*.*ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ At Acumen Learning, he developed the Five Business Drivers model (Cash, Profit, Assets, Growth, and People).ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ The model’s stated goal is “to help companies to improve employee engagement” and “to increase their business acumen”.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ


## Career

In 2002, Cope partnered with Stephen M.R. Covey and Ram Charan to found Acumen Learning, a leadership training company. At Acumen Learning, Cope developed the Five Business Drivers model, which has been implemented by 18 of the Fortune 50 and is used to train more than 30,000 people in more than 30 countries annually. In 2011, Acumen Learning was ranked No. 1631 on the Inc. 5000 and in 2012, it received the Business of the Year Award from ISA – The Association of Learning Providers.

On March 4, 2012, Cope published *Seeing the Big Picture: Business Acumen to Build Your Credibility, Career, and Company*, which reached fourth place on the New York Times Best Seller List, the first place on *The Wall Street Journal* Best Seller List, and the first place on *USA Today* Best Seller List. He has also contributed articles to Fast Company, Chief Executive, TLNT, and Training Magazine.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ

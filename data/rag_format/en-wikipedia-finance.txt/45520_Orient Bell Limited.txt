# Orient Bell Limited



**Orient Bell Limited** is an Indian manufacturer of ceramic and vitrified tiles. The company was founded in 1977 and is headquartered in New Delhi. It is publicly traded on both the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) and the National Stock Exchange (NSE) under the ticker symbol ORIENTBELL.


## History

Orient Bell Limited was incorporated on May 18, 1977, and began commercial production later that year. In 2012, the company merged with Bell Ceramics, expanding its product offerings and market presence in India. The company has established several tile display centers across India, branded as Orient Bell Tile Boutiques (OBTBs), to showcase its product range.


## Manufacturing Facilities

Orient Bell operates several manufacturing plants located in Sikandrabad (Uttar Pradesh), Hoskote (Karnataka), and Dora (Gujarat). These facilities have a combined capacity to produce millions of square meters of tiles annually. The company has invested in modern machinery and technology to meet production demands and adhere to environmental regulations.


## Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

As part of its Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives, Orient Bell participates in community development programs, supports education, and engages in sustainability efforts. The company works to improve the communities around its manufacturing facilities and promote environmentally friendly production practices.

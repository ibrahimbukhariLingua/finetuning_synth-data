# Bluebird International



**Bluebird International** is a midsize Hungarian private information technology staffing and software development company headquartered in Budapest with offices in Dublin (Ireland), Miami (USA), and Vienna (Austria).
Bluebird International was founded in 2001 by Balazs Refi, an IT project manager and business analyst.
The Budapest headquartered company has hundreds of clients including several Fortune 500 companies and is known for its labour market survey and tech market research in Hungary.
In January 2024 the founding CEO Balazs Refi was replaced by Viktoria Hradek. For fiscal year 2023 the Hungary unit posted over EUR 8.8 milion.
Since its foundation, the company has been a one-person enterprise. By 2024, the company grew into an over 50-person strong head office, with about 120 IT professionals in Hungary and 10 in Austria, and had completed more than 1,000,000 engineering hours of project work by 2020.


## History

Bluebird International started as a one-person company in an apartment office in Budapest, Hungary back in 2001.
During early 2000s, the company executed development projects in IT, after which it slowly expanded operations into other fields. It started to hire tech professionals in addition to Time and Materials in 2005 and by 2007 it had begun recruitment services.

Well into 2012, the firm had started its second international expansion after Germany, entering into neighboring Austria under the name *Bluebird Personalberatungs GmbH* In Austria, it hired English speaking Europeans in its office at Vienna and collaborated with T-Systems, Raiffeisen Bank, Erste Bank, BAWAG, Finanzamt, AGES, Payolution, Hutchinson Drei Austria, Scout24.ⁿᵒⁿ-ᵖʳⁱᵐᵃʳʸ ˢᵒᵘʳᶜᵉ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ

By 2020, it started fixed-price IT Solutions including web development, applications development, and other services.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ

In March 2023, the company moved to its new office headquarters at the business corridor of Budapest at the H2Offices on Váci Road.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ

In April 2023, in order to set up presence in North America and the United States, and thus globally by setting Bluebird Global Inc., they opened their first offices in North America, in Florida, USA later expanding their workspaces to Dublin, Ireland in September.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ

From 2024, Viktoria Hradek replaced the founder Balazas Refi as the CEO.

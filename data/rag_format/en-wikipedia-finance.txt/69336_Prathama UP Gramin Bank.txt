# Prathama UP Gramin Bank



**Prathama UP Gramin Bank** was created on 1 April 2019 by the amalgamation of two regional rural banks in Uttar Pradesh:

* Prathama Bank
* Sarva UP Gramin Bank

The creation of Prathama UP Gramin Bank took place under Sub-Section (1) of Section 23A of the Regional Rural Bank Act, 1976 (21 of 1976).

The bank has 950 branches around 19 districts of Uttar Pradesh; it will also have one branch in Haridwar District of Uttarakhand. The bank is sponsored by the Punjab National Bank.


## Sarva UP Gramin Bank

Previously PUPGB was Sarva UP Gramin Bank (SUPGB). SUPGB was created in 2013 by amalgamation of 4 RRBs viz. Uttar Pradesh Gramin Bank Meerut, Kisan Gramin Bank Budaun, Rani Laxmi Bai Kshetriya Gramin Bank Jhansi and Devipatan Kshetriya Gramin Bank Gonda under subsection (1) of section 23 A of Regional Rural Bank Act, 1976.


## Prathama Bank

Prathama Bank (lit. First Bank) was established in 1975 by Syndicate Bank. It was the first RRB in India.

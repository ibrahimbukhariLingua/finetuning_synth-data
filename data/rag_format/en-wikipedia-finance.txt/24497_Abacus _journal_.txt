# Abacus (journal)



***Abacus*** is a peer-reviewed quarterly academic journal published by Wiley-Blackwell on behalf of the Accounting Foundation (University of Sydney).


## Overview

It was established in 1965. The editors-in-chief are Stewart Jones (University of Sydney) and Baljit K. Sidhu (University of Sydney). According to the *Journal Citation Reports*, the journal has a 2023 impact factor of 2.5.

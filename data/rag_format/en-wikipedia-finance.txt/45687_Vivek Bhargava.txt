# Vivek Bhargava



**Vivek Bhargava** (born 1977) is an Indian entrepreneur and a figure in the digital marketing and technology sectors. He is the co-founder of a SaaS startup.


## Early life and education

Vivek Bhargava was born in 1977 in India. Growing up in a business family, Bhargava was exposed to entrepreneurship from a young age, which later inspired his ventures in the digital domain. He completed his education in Mumbai, where he also laid the foundation for his career in digital marketing.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ


## Career

Bhargava's entrepreneurial journey began in 1997 when he founded **Communicate 2**, one of India’s pioneering digital marketing agencies. Under his leadership, Communicate 2 grew to become a leading performance marketing agency in the country. In 2012, the company was acquired by **Dentsu International**, and Bhargava took on the role of founder and managing director of **iProspect Communicate 2**. Following the acquisition, iProspect Communicate 2 was rebranded as **iProspect India**.

In 2016, Bhargava was appointed CEO of the **Dentsu Aegis Network (DAN) Performance Group**.

In 2020, Bhargava co-founded **consumr.ai (formerly ProfitWheel)**, a SaaS startup.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ

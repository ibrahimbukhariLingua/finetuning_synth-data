# 4Frontiers Corporation



**4Frontiers Corporation** is an American space commerce company, founded in 2005.


## History

In 2014, 4Frontiers began a private placement round of financing to raise US$15–20 million to fund part of US$80 million required to build the first phase of INTERSPACE Florida, "a real science interactive space themed destination" on a 75 acres (30 ha) parcel of land near Titusville Cocoa Airport.

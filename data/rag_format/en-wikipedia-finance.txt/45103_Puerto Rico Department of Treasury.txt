# Puerto Rico Department of Treasury



The **Puerto Rico Department of Treasury** (Spanish: *Departamento de Hacienda de Puerto Rico*) is the executive department of the government of Puerto Rico responsible for the treasury of the U.S. Commonwealth of Puerto Rico. It is one of the constitutionally-created executive departments and is headed by a Secretary.

The department collects taxes, operates the local lottery, and serves as the central disbursement agency of the government.

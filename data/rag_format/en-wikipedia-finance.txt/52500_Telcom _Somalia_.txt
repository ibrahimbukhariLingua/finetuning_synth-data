# Telcom (Somalia)



**Telcom** is a telecommunications network operator in Somalia. It is the first major privately owned company providing telecommunications services to cities nationwide.


## Overview

TELCOM SOMALIA is headquartered in Bakaara Market, Mogadishu. It has representative offices in Dubai, UAE and in London, UK, where accounting, international relations and carrier services are handled.

Its chairman is Mohamed Sheikh, with Said Olow as General Manager, and Hassan Ibrahim Mursal serving as CTO and Omar Hussein Adan as chief transmission officer. There are 750 staff employees.
It has three dependent networks in Mogadishu, Hargeisa and Bosasso - while there is an interconnectivity link between all of them

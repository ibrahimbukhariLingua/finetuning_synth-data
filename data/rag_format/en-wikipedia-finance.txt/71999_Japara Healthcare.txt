# Japara Healthcare



**Japara Healthcare** is an Australian company that operates aged care homes in Australia. It was listed on the Australian Securities Exchange until November 2021 when it was acquired by Calvary Care.


## History

In March 2018, the company acquired the Riviera Health residential aged care portfolio - four aged care facilities at Brighton-Le-Sands, Chatswood, Doonside and Wyong, as well as a closed aged care facility in Toukley.

Calvary Health Care acquired Japara in November 2021 for a reported AUD 380 million.

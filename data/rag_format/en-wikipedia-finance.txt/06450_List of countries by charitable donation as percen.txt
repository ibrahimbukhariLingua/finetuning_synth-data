# List of countries by charitable donation as percentage of GDP



The following **list of countries by charitable donation** shows the total charitable donations from individuals within the nation, as a percentage of the nation's GDP.

The figures were published in February 2016 by the Charities Aid Foundation (CAF) in its report titled *Gross Domestic Philanthropy*. The report only considers the 24 countries about which CAF was able to collect comprehensive data.

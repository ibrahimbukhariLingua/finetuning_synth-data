# Stockade Companies



**Stockade Companies, LLC** is a privately held multi-concept restaurant company based in Round Rock, Texas. Founded in 1984, it operates under three brands, Sirloin Stockade, Montana Mike's, and WingMe. Stockade Companies operates 16 restaurants throughout Illinois, Indiana, Iowa, Kansas, Missouri, North Dakota, Oklahoma, and Texas.


## History

The company was established in 1984 by a group of Sirloin Stockade franchisees.

In 2008, Tom Ford became the CEO of Stockade Companies. Ford began working for the company on its first day in 1966 as a dishwasher at the debut restaurant in Oklahoma City. Ford worked in additional positions while attending college and later purchased a Sirloin Stockade restaurant in 1979 in the Austin, Texas area.

On June 2, 2020, the last Coyote Canyon restaurant, located in Bradley, Illinois, closed due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

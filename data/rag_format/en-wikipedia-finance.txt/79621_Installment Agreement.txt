# Installment Agreement



An **Instalment Agreement** is a United States Internal Revenue Service (IRS) program that allows individuals to pay tax debt in monthly payments.

There IRS has several different kinds of Instalment Agreements; *Guaranteed*, *Streamline*, *Partial* and *Full Pay*. There are a number of requirements that have to be met before an instalment agreement can be granted to a tax payer.


## Requirements

* Has to file all minimum required tax returns.
* Must owe $50,000 or less.
* Monthly payment cannot be less than $25 a month.
* Keep a check on future refunds that can applied for tax refunds until tax debt is paid in full.

# Unity Foods



**Unity Foods Limited**, formerly known as **Taha Spinning Mills**, is a Pakistani food company headquartered in Karachi. It is a subsidiary of Singaporean multinational company, Wilmar International.

Unity Foods produces staples (flour, rice, lentils, pulses), industrial fats and various feed ingredients for poultry and livestock sector.


## History

Unity Foods was founded as Taha Spinning Mills in 1991.

In 2018, Unity Foods changed its business from textile to the production of edible oil and refining.

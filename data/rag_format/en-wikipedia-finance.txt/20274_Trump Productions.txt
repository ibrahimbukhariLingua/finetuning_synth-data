# Trump Productions



**Trump Productions LLC** was an American television production company established by Donald Trump that served as the entertainment business wing of the Trump Organization. It was founded in 2004 and ceased operations in 2015. The company produced network and cable television shows including *The Apprentice* and *Celebrity Apprentice* in association with Mark Burnett Productions. The firm also produced the Miss USA and Miss Universe pageants. Financial disclosures made during the Trump campaign valued the business at $15 million, with revenue over $4 million in 2015.

Donald Trump served as the chairman and CEO of the company, which was based in Los Angeles, California.


## Productions

* The Apprentice (U.S.) (2004–2015)
* *Pageant Place* (2007)
* *Celebrity Apprentice* (2008–2017)
* *Donald J. Trump Presents The Ultimate Merger* (2010–2011)

# Journal of Marketing Management



The ***Journal of Marketing Management*** is a peer-reviewed academic journal covering the field of marketing. The journal is owned by Westburn Publishers and published by Routledge. The editor-in-chief is Mark Tadajewski (University of York). It is the official journal of the United Kiingdom's Academy of Marketing and was established in 1985 by Michael J. Baker (University of Strathclyde).


## Abstracting and indexing

The journal is abstracted and indexed in Scopus and the Social Sciences Citation Index. According to the *Journal Citation Reports*, its 2023 impact factor is 3.5.

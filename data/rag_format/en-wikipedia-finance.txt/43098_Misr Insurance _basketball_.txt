# Misr Insurance (basketball)



**Misr Insurance** (Arabic: مصر للتأمين), sometimes translated as **Egypt Insurance**, is an Egyptian basketball club based in Alexandria. The team plays in the Egyptian Basketball Super League, the highest level league in the country.

The team is owned by and named after the insurance company Misr Life Insurance.

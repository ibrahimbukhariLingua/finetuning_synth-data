# List of banks in Cambodia



This is a **list of banks** in Cambodia.

As of July 31, 2020ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, there were 51 commercial banks, 14 specialized banks, 75 microfinance institutions, 7 microfinance deposit-taking institutions, 6 representative offices, and 15 leasing companies in Cambodia.

As of September 30, 2021ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, there were 54 commercial banks, 10 specialized banks, 79 microfinance institutions, 6 microfinance deposit-taking institutions, 6 representative offices, and 17 financial leasing companies in Cambodia.

As of June 20, 2022, National Bank of Cambodia here are 58 commercial banks in Cambodia.

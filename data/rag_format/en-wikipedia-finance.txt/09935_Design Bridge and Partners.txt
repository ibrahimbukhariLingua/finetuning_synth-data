# Design Bridge and Partners



**Design Bridge and Partners**, known as **Design Bridge and Partners Worldwide Limited** legally, is a global brand and design consultancy. Founded after the merger of Superunion and Design Bridge in April 2023, it is owned by WPP plc.


## History

In July 2022, WPP announced the merger of Superunion and Design Bridge, two design consultancies. The company was intended to launch in January 2023, however it was delayed to April 2023. The company that was used for Superunion was legally renamed to Design Bridge and Partners Worldwide Limited in February 2023, 2 months before the official launch.

# Charles Goode



**Charles Barrington Goode** AC (born 26 August 1938) is an Australian director of public companies.


## Early life and education

Goode attended Scotch College, Melbourne and is a graduate of the University of Melbourne, where he was a resident of Ormond College, completing a Bachelor of Commerce (Hons). He also received an MBA and LLD (Hons) from that University and an LLD (Hons) from Monash University.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ


## Career

After an early career at the establishment stockbroking firm Potter Partners, he has had a long career as a director and Chairman of major public companies. His positions have included:ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ

* Chairman of ANZ Bank (1995-2010)
* Chairman of Woodside Petroleum (1999-2007) (non-executive director since 1988)
* Director of Singapore Airlines (1999-2006)
* Chairman of Australian United Investment Company Limited
* Chairman of Diversified United Investment Limited
* Chairman of the Ian Potter Foundation
* President of the Howard Florey Institute of Experimental Physiology and Medicine (1997-2004; director since 1987)ʷʰᵉⁿ?

## Honours

He became a Companion of the Order of Australia in 2001.

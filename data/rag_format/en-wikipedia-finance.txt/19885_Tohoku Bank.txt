# Tohoku Bank



**The Tohoku Bank Limited** (株式会社東北銀行, *Kabushiki-gaisha Tōhoku Ginkō*) is a Japanese regional bank that is based out of Morioka, the capital of Iwate prefecture in the Tohoku region of Japan. It is one of the smallest regional banks in Japan.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ


## History

The bank was created in November, 1950.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ

# TR Property Investment Trust



**TR Property Investment Trust** is a large British investment trust dedicated to investments in the property sector. Established in 1905, the company is a constituent of the FTSE 250 Index. It is managed by Thames River Capital LLP. By 2024, Thames River Capital LLP was part of Columbia Threadneedle Investments.


## History

The company was formed in 1905 as **The Trust Union** consolidating several smaller investment trust companies. On 5 April 1982, the company changed its investment policy to that of "investing predominately in securities in the property sector". At the same time it changed its name to **TR Property Investment Trust PLC**.

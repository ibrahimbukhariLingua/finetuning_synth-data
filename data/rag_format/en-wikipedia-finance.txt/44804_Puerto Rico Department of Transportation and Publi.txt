The **Department of Transportation and Public Works** (**DTOP**; Spanish: *Departamento de Transportación y Obras Públicas*) is the Executive Department of the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico that regulates transportation and public works in Puerto Rico. The agency's headquarters are located in San Juan.


## Agencies

The following agencies are involved in transportation issues of Puerto Rico.

# Gamone Pwint



**Ga Mone Pwint** (Burmese: ဂမုန်းပွင့်; **GMP**) is a Burmese conglomerate. Ga Mone Pwint is presently headed by Managing Director Nay Lynn Oo. The company runs major shopping centers in Yangon.


## Stores

* Myaynigone Ga Mone Pwint
* Bokalay Ga Mone Pwint
* Thein Gyi Zay Ga Mone Pwint
* Kaba Aye Ga Mone Pwint
* San Yeik Nyein Ga Mone Pwint
* Kan Taw Lay Ga Mone Pwint (under construction)

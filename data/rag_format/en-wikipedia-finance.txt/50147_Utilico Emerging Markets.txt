# Utilico Emerging Markets



**Utilico Emerging Markets Trust plc** (LSE: UEM) is a large British investment trust dedicated to investments in infrastructure, utility and related sectors in the emerging markets. Established in 2005, Utilico Emerging Markets Trust ('UEM') is a listed on the London Stock Exchange. The Chairman is John Rennocks.

UEM investments focuses predominantly on the emerging markets of Asia, Latin America, Emerging Europe and Africa. UEM is managed by ICM Limited and ICM Investment Management Limited, which is authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority.

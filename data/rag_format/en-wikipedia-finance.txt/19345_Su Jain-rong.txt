# Su Jain-rong



**Su Jain-rong** (Chinese: 蘇建榮; pinyin: *Sū Jiànróng*; born 12 November 1961) is a Taiwanese politician who served as the finance minister from 2018 to 2022.


## Education

Su obtained his master's degree in economics from National Chung Hsing University and doctoral degree in economics from Pennsylvania State University in the United States.


## Political careers

Su was appointed the Political Deputy Minister of Finance on 20 May 2016. On 12 July 2018, it was announced that Su would replace Sheu Yu-jer as the Minister of Finance starting on 16 July 2018.

# OpenPayd



**OpenPayd** is a London-based banking-as-a-service platform that provides financial services for businesses through a single API.


## History

OpenPayd was founded in London in 2018 by Ozan Özerk, the founder of European Merchant Bank, MSB.US, and Ozan SuperApp. It initially focused on payments and banking for businesses through modular technology and a global license network. The company expanded its operations internationally from the UK, opening offices in US, Canada, Malta, Bulgaria, Turkey and France.

In 2020, OpenPayd launched banking and payment services for digital currency businesses, allowing them to access banking and payment networks.

In 2022, OpenPayd began offering Open Banking across the UK and Europe. In 2023, OpenPayd was "Highly Commended" in the Paytech Company of the Year category at the Fintech Awards London.


## Operations

OpenPayd gives access to Faster Payments, SEPA, SEPA Instant, SWIFT, CHAPS, Bacs, ACH, PIX, Fedwire, etc. OpenPayd’s FX services give automated spot-pricing and instant settlements for businesses operating in multiple currencies.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ

The platform serves various industries, including financial services, brokerage, and digital assets.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ Customers include eToro, OKX, Libertex, B2C2, Bitfinex and Caxton. OpenPayd processes over €50 billion in annual payment volume.

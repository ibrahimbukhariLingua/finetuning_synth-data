# Yazd Tire



**Yazd rubber industries complex** (Persian: مجتمع صنایع لاستیک یزد, *Mijitim'-e Sânai'-ye Lâstik-e Yezd*), also known as **Yazd Tire** (یزد تایر), is an Iranian tire manufacturer for automobiles, commercial trucks, light trucks, SUVs, race cars, airplanes, and heavy earth-mover machinery. It has been established since 1985.


## Operations

Yazd Tire is a joint-stock company; Artawheel Tire and Social Security Investment Group are the company's largest shareholders.

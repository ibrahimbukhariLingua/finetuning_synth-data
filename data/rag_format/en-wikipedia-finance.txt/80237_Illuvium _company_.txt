# Illuvium (company)



**Illuvium** is a blockchain game developer. Iluvium has created three games, *Illuvium Overworld*, *Illuvium Arena*, and *Illuvium Zero* which operate via the Ethereum blockchain.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ


## History

Co-founded by Kieran Warwick in September 2020, Illuvium began developing a blockchain-based gaming platform. The game *Illuvium* combines features of an open-world role-playing game (RPG) with an autobattler and a monster-taming game. It's intended to cater to both casual gamers and decentralized finance (DeFi) users.

# CityMall (Philippines)



**CityMall Commercial Centers, Inc.**, simply known as **CityMall**, is a Philippine community shopping mall chain formed as a subsidiary of DoubleDragon Properties. As of 2023, it has 43 branches across the Philippines.


## History

The CityMall brand was launched in late 2015, with CityMall Arnaldo, Roxas as its first mall.

In November 2016, Grand CityMall Mandalagan, Bacolod made its grand opening, making it the first Grand CityMall to open.

The **Ministry of Finance** (Tajik: Вазорати молияи Ҷумҳурии Тоҷикистон) is the Tajikistani government ministry which oversees the public finances of Tajikistan.


## Ministers responsible for finance

* Normat Unusov, 1991-1992
* Ismail Davlatov, 1992-1994
* Mahmad Yusunov, 1994
* Anvarsho Muzaffarov, 1994-2000
* Safarali Najmuddinov [ru], 2000-2013
* Abdusalom Kurbonov, 2013-2018
* Faiziddin Qahhorzoda, 2018-

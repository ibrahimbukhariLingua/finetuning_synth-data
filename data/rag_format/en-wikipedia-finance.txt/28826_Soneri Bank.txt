# Soneri Bank



**Soneri Bank Limited** (Urdu pronunciation: [so.ˈneː.ri bɛŋk] so-NAY-ree-BANK) is a Pakistani bank based in Karachi. Its head office is located in the PNSC Building. Soneri Bank started its operation in the year 1992.


## Leadership

Amin A. Feerasta is Chairman of the Bank.

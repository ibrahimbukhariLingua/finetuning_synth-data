# BRW Rich 200, 2012



The ***BRW* Rich 200, 2012** is the 29th annual survey of the wealthiest people resident in Australia, published in hardcopy and online formats in the *BRW* magazine on 24 May 2012.

In the 2012 list, the net worth of the wealthiest individual, Gina Rinehart, was A$29.17 billion.

The *BRW Rich Families List* was published annually since 2008. In the 2012 list, the Smorgon family headed the list with estimated wealth of A$2.63 billion. The Smorgon families headed the families list in every year of its publication. The families list was last published in 2015.

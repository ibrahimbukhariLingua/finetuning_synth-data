# Capital Mall (Missouri)



**Capital Mall** is a shopping mall located in Jefferson City, Missouri, United States that opened in 1978. Its anchors are JCPenney and Dillard's. Sears closed in March 2017.

In 2013, the mall's owner, Farmer Holding Company, applied to Jefferson City for approval to build a 127-room hotel and 61,000 square feet (5,700 m²) conference center at Capital Mall. Each would cost approximately $14 million. It was never built.

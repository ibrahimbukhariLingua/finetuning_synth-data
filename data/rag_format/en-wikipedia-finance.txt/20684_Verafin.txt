# Verafin



**Verafin Inc.** is a Canadian fraud detection technology and anti-money laundering software company based in St. John's, Newfoundland and Labrador, Canada. The company was established in 2003 by Jamie King, Brendan Brothers, and Raymond Pretty.


## Growth and development

More than 1,200 credit unions in the United States use Verafin to effectively fight financial crime. In July 2012, Verafin employed around 130 employees at their St. John's headquarters and Birmingham, Alabama office. It's reportedᵇʸ ʷʰᵒᵐ? that the company serves around 2,000 clients—mostly small and medium-size banks. On November 19, 2020, Nasdaq, Inc. announced a deal to buy the company for US$2.75 billion, promising to maintain Verafin's existing headquarters in St. John's. The deal was completed in February 2021.

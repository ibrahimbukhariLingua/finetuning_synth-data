# Sheikh Bashir Uddin



**Sheikh Bashir Uddin** (Bengali: সেখ বসির উদ্দিন) is a Bangladeshi businessman and the managing director of Akij-Bashir Group, an industrial conglomerate in Bangladesh. He is currently serving as an adviser to the 2024 interim government of Bangladesh.


## Early life

Bashir Uddin was born in 1947 in the village of Navaron in Jessore District. He belonged to a Bengali Muslim family originally from the village of Madhyadanga in Phultala, Khulna District. His father, Sheikh Akij Uddin, was the founding chairman of Akij Group.


## Career

He took oath as an advisor on 10 November 2024.

# Forrester Research



**Forrester** a research and advisory firm. Forrester serves clients in North America, Europe, and Asia Pacific. The firm is headquartered in Cambridge, MA with global offices in Amsterdam, London, New Delhi, New York, NY, Norwalk, CT, San Francisco, Singapore, Stockholm, and Sydney.


## History and Key Milestones

* Forrester was founded in July 1983 by George Forrester Colony in Cambridge, MA.

## Leadership

* George F. Colony, chairman of the board and CEO.

# Dongbei University of Finance and Economics Press



**Dongbei University of Finance and Economics Press** (**DUFEP**; Chinese: 东北财经大学出版社; *Dongbei* means Northeast) is a university press owned by Dongbei University of Finance and Economics, located in Dalian, Liaoning Province, Northeast China.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ

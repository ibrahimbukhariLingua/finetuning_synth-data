# BBGI



**BBGI Global Infrastructure** is a Luxembourg-based publicly listed investment company dedicated to infrastructure investments. It has headquarters in Luxembourg. It is a constituent of the FTSE 250 Index.


## History

Established in 2011, as *Bilfinger Berger Global Infrastructure*, the company adopted the name *BBGI SICAV SA* in 2014 before adopting BBGI Global Infrastructure.

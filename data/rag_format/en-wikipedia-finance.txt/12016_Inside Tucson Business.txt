# Inside Tucson Business



***Inside Tucson Business*** is a weekly newspaper published in Tucson that covers the business, financial, and economic news of Southern Arizona.


## History

Wick Communications from 1992 to 2014, when it was sold to 10/13 Communications. In 2021, Times Media Group acquired the Tucson publications of 10/13 Communications (including *The Explorer*, the *Marana News*, *Foothills News*, *Desert Times*, *Tucson Weekly*, and *Inside Tucson Business*).

# Sibirtelecom



**Sibirtelecom** (Russian: ОАО «Сибирьтелеком»), a former subsidiary of Svyazinvest Holding Company, is a regional telecommunications and Internet service provider in Siberia. It is responsible for providing service to over 29% of Russia's territory.


## History

The company was formed by the legal union of many regional service providers. Please see the list of subsidiaries below.

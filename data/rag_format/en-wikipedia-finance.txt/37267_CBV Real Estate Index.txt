# CBV Real Estate Index



The **CBV Real Estate Index** is the first index on the Vietnamese real estate market. It is built based on the newest methodology of S&P for that market.


## Types of indices

CBV real estate index can be categorized into following 7 smaller indexes:

1. CBV Vietnam Real Estate Composite Index
2. CBV Hanoi Real Estate Index
3. CBV Ho Chi Minh City Real Estate Index
4. CBV Vietnam Office Index
5. CBV Vietnam Apartment Index
6. CBV Vietnam Retail Index
7. CBV Vietnam Warehouse Index

# Kwame Addo-Kufuor Jnr



**Kwame Addo-Kufuor Jnr** was a Ghanaian finance and mining executive who served as the president of the Ghana Chamber of Mines and the first president of the General Assembly of the ECOWAS Federation of Chambers of Mines (EFEDCOM).


## Early life and education

Addo-Kufuor Jnr was born to Kwame Addo-Kufuor. He holds a Master of Business Administration (MBA) from Henley Business School with first-class degree in Business Administration from the University of Ghana. Addo-Kufuor Jnr was a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants in England and Wales and a member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Ghana

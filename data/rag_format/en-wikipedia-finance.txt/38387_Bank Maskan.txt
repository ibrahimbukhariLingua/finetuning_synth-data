# Bank Maskan



**Bank Maskan** (Persian: بانک مسکن, *Bank Mesken*), also known as the **Housing Bank**, is a bank in Iran. In 2008, the Central Bank banned all banks and other financial institutions, except for Maskan Bank, from providing residential mortgages.


## Structure

Their subsidiaries include:

* **Housing Investment Company** (شرکت سرمایه گذاری مسکن; *Šerkat Sarmāye Gozāri Maskan*), established 1991; they received the Iran National Quality Award (جايزه ملي كيفيت ايران) in 2004

## History

A new CEO Mahmood Shayan (محمود شایان) was appointed in August 2020.

# ESL



At UCLouvain (Université catholique de Louvain, Louvain-la-Neuve, Belgium), the Economics School of Louvain (ESL) is the entity specializing in economics and offering programmes at the undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral levels. The ESL is renowned for its excellence in teaching, research, and global perspective.


## Ranking

ESL programmes are ranked 19ᵗʰ in the world out of 17,000 programmes in 154 countries by Eduniversal, and 77ᵗʰ and 28ᵗʰ in the EU in the QS 2021 rankings.


## History

ESL's masters are organised jointly with the Economics School of Namur (ESN).


## Programmes

Undergraduate/Bac

* Jointly with the Louvain School of Management (LSM), a bachelor in Economics and Business

Graduate/Master

* Master in Economics (professional track) (2 years, 120 ECTS)
* Master in Economics (research track) (2 years, 120 ECTS)
* International double-degree masters in partnership with :

1. Bocconi University (Milan): General Economics (Research track)
2. Maastricht University : Sustainable and Applied Economics (Profession track)
3. Università degli Studi di Milano : Economics & Politics (Profession track)
4. Università degli Studi di Milano : Economics & Finance (Research track)
5. Universidade Catolica Portuguesa (Lisboa): General Economics (Research track)
6. EAFID (Medellin) : General Economics (Professional track)
7. EAFID (Medellin) : Economics and Finance (Professional and research track)
8. Université de Strasbourg : European Economics (Professional track)

* QEM Erasmus Mundus master in Quantitative Economics

DOCTORAL level

* PhD in Economics

## Research

Research at the ESL takes place in the following centres of excellence:

* CORE : Center for operations research and econometrics
* IRES : Institut de recherches économiques et sociales
* ETES : Chaire Hoover d'éthique économique et sociale.
* CIRTES: Centre Interdisciplinaire de Recherche, Travail, État et Société

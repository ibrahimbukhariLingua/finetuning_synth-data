# 3i Infrastructure



**3i Infrastructure plc** is an investment trust headquartered in Jersey. It is listed on the London Stock Exchange and is a constituent of the FTSE 250 Index.


## History

The company was launched by way of an initial public offering in 2007. Its early investments included Anglian Water Group and Oiltanking GmbH's terminal facilities in the Netherlands, Malta, and Singapore. Between 2010 and 2020, 3i Infrastructure invested in businesses such as Esvagt, Infinis and Wireless Infrastructure Group, a UK mobile infrastructure provider.

# Bhubaneswar ITIR



The **Bhubaneswar ITIR** is one of the planned 40 *"Information Technology Investment Regions"* in India. It is proposed over an area of about 40.5 km² (10000 acres) in Bhubaneswar in the region encompassing Infocity-I and Infocity-II. The Detailed Project Report (DPR) is being prepared by IL&FS Infrastructures Ltd in collaboration with the state-owned Industrial Infrastructure Development Corp (IDCO).


## Timeline

* ITIR reduced by 6–7 km² due to the proposed IIT and International Airport.

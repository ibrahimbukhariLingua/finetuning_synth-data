# Zurich Kotak General Insurance



**Zurich Kotak General Insurance Company** is an Indian general insurance company headquartered in Mumbai. It offers non-life insurance products like motor, car, bike, health, travel, Home insurance and others. It is 70% owned by Swiss insurance company Zurich Insurance.


## History

In April 2015, Kotak General Insurance received the license to conduct general insurance business in India from the Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (IRDAI).

By September 2020, the business it had grown to have a national footprint of 19 branches spread across India.

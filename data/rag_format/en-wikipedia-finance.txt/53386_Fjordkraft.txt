# Fjordkraft



**Fjordkraft** is a Norwegian power company that delivers power to end users.


## Overview

The company took over the retailing function from BKK and Skagerak Energi, which along with Statkraft own the company. Power is bought on Nord Pool Spot. The power grid and production facilities are retained by the owners. The company is based in Bergen, with its other two regional offices in Sandefjord and Trondheim.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ


## Partnerships

ONZO, a London-based Data Science Software Company announced a 5-year partnership with Fjordkraft, in 2015.

# Thomas Kingston



**Thomas Kingston** was a British financier and a member of the extended royal family. He was married to Lady Gabriella Windsor, the daughter of Prince and Princess Michael of Kent. Kingston had a notable career in finance, working as a director at Devonport Capital. He also had a background in economic history, having studied at Bristol University.

Kingston was known for his connections to the royal family and his previous relationship with Pippa Middleton. He died at the age of 45 due to a traumatic head wound. His death garnered great public attention, and sympathy, from both the Royal Family and the general public.

In 2019, Kingston married Lady Gabriella Windsor, the daughter of Prince and Princess Michael of Kent. ## Death

The death of Thomas Kingston, aged 45, was announced by Buckingham Palace on 27 February 2024. At an inquest opening at Gloucestershire coroner's court, it was revealed that Kingston had died from a "traumatic head wound" and a gun had been found near his body in an outbuilding at his parents' home in the Cotswolds. The police believed the death was not suspicious.

A private funeral took place at the Chapel Royal in St James's Palace on 12 March. About 140 friends and family members, including Prince and Princess Michael of Kent, and the Prince of Wales, attended the service.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ

# Chad P. Bown



**Chad P. Bown** is an American economist. He served as chief economist at the U.S. Department of State in the Biden administration from January 2024 to January 2025.


## Education

Bown holds a BA in economics and international relations from Bucknell University and a PhD in economics from the University of Wisconsin, Madison.


## Career

Bown was a senior fellow at the Peterson Institute for International Economics (PIIE) from 2016 to 2023. Prior to joining PIIE he served as senior economist for international trade and investment on the White House Council of Economic Advisers and lead economist at the World Bank.

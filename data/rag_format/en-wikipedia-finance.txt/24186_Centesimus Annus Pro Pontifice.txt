# Centesimus Annus Pro Pontifice



**Centesimus Annus Pro Pontifice** (**CAPP**) (it: *Fondazione Centesimus Annus – Pro Pontifice*) is a pontifical foundation.

Pope John Paul II established the "Fondazione Centesimus Annus - Pro Pontifice" on 13 June 1993 along with lay Catholic business, academic and professional leaders. The Foundation is a lay-led non-profit organisation. Its purpose is to promote the Catholic social doctrine, especially as expressed in the encyclical *Centesimus Annus* (1991) and the aims of the Apostolic See. It is headquartered in Vatican City.

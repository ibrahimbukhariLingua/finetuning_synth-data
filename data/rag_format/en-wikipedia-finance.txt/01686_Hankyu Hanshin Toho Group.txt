# Hankyu Hanshin Toho Group



The **Hankyu Hanshin Toho Group** (阪急阪神東宝グループ, *Hankyū Hanshin Tōhō Gurūpu*) (formerly known as the *Hankyu Toho Group* (阪急東宝グループ, *Hankyū Tōhō Gurūpu*) before 2006, is a Japanese keiretsu (association of businesses) centered around companies established by railway tycoon Ichizō Kobayashi: Hankyu Hanshin Holdings, H₂O Retailing and Toho. The keiretsu historically included Sanwa Bank, now part of Mitsubishi UFJ Financial Group, and is still closely linked to MUFG; it also has close ties with the Fujisankei Communications Group.

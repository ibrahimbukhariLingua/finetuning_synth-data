# IShares S&P 100



**iShares S&P 100 Index Fund** is an exchange-traded fund of US stocks. The fund tracks the S&P 100 index.

Its ticker is OEF.


## Holdings

Top ten holdings:

* Apple Inc. (AAPL)
* Exxon Mobil Corp. (XOM)
* International Business Machines (IBM)
* AT&T Inc. (T)
* Microsoft Corp. (MSFT)
* Citigroup Inc. (C)
* Chevron Corp. (CVX)
* Johnson & Johnson (JNJ)
* Coca-Cola Co. (KO)
* Pfizer Inc. (PFE)

(as of June 2012)


## Competitors

iShares S&P 100 mainly competes with S&P 500 funds, including SPDR S&P 500 (NYSE Arca: SPY), iShares Core S&P 500 (NYSE Arca: IVV), and Vanguard S&P 500 ETF (NYSE Arca: VOO). Those funds are not mega; one fund that is more similar is Guggenheim Russell Top 50 Mega Cap (NYSE Arca: IWL).

# Vice President of Iran for Economic Affairs



**Vice President for Economic Affairs** (Persian: معاون اقتصادی رئیس‌جمهور) is a government position in Iran whose officeholder acts as a Vice President of Iran. Mohsen Rezaee formerly served in the position, being appointed on 25 August 2021 to 11 June 2023.

As existence of this office is not obligatory by law, the responsibilities and authorities vested in this position could vary and many presidents did not appoint anyone in the capacity.

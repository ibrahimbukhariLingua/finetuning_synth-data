# Plug-in electric vehicles in Malta



As of March 2022ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, there were 6,000 electric vehicles registered in Malta.


## Government policy

As of October 2021ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, the Maltese government offers subsidies of up to €12,000 for electric vehicle purchases. As of January 2023ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, electric and plug-in hybrid vehicles with an electric range of at least 50 kilometres (31 mi) are exempt from registration tax.

As of January 2023ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, 7% of vehicles in the national government fleet were electric.


## Charging stations

As of October 2021ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, the Maltese government offers subsidies of up to €900 for charging station installation.


## Public opinion

In a 2022 poll conducted by the European Investment Bank, 36% of respondents said that they would buy an electric car for their next vehicle purchase.

# Ultatel



**Ultatel** is an American telecommunications company founded in 2016 by Amr Ibrahim. Headquartered in Herndon, Virginia, United States, the company operates globally and specializes in cloud-based communication.


## History

Ultatel was established in 2016 in Herndon, Virginia by Amr Ibrahim. In January 2021, the company was certified by Microsoft to integrate Session Border Control (SBC) through Microsoft Teams. In September 2023, the company also received Microsoft certification for its Contact Center Software, allowing Ultatel's contact center customers integrate their contact center and internal collaboration with Microsoft Teams.

In 2023 and 2024, Ultatel was placed in the Inc. 5000 list as one of the fastest-growing private companies in the United States. ## Operations

ULTATEL is a provider of unified communications software. It offers an integrated platform that delivers cloud-based Voice over Internet protocol (VoIP) technology. The platform supports voice, SMS, fax, chat, and video. Ultatel cloud-based communication products include the Clarity Cloud Phone System, Contact Center Software, MS Teams Direct Routing SBC, and MS Teams Certified Contact Center. ULTATEL provides Transport Layer Security (TLS) and Secure Real-Time Transport Protocol (SRTP) encryption between all endpoints. The company is headquartered in Herndon, Virginia, and employs 220 people. Ultatel is led by its founder and CEO, Amr Ibrahim.

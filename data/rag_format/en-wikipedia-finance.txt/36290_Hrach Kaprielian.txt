# Hrach Kaprielian



**Hrach Kaprielian** (born 31 August 1953) is an American-based Armenian businessman, known for serving as the chairman of the board of Artsakhbank, and the co-owner and president of FC Ararat Yerevan, a football club based in Yerevan, Armenia.


## Career

In 1979, Kaprielian founded Kaprielian Enterprises, a jewellery manufacturing and design company. Subsequently, Kaprielian went on to serve as the chairman of the board of Artsakhbank, an Armenian-based bank headquartered in Yerevan, Armenia.

Kaprielian is the president of FC Ararat Yerevan, an Armenian football club which plays in the Armenian Premier League.

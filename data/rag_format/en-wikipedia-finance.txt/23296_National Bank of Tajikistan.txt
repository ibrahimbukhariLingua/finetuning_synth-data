# National Bank of Tajikistan



The **National Bank of Tajikistan** (Tajik: Бонки миллии Тоҷикистон) is the central bank of Tajikistan.

The Bank is engaged in developing policies to promote financial inclusion and is a member of the Alliance for Financial Inclusion. Deposits in the National Bank of Tajikistan during the first half of 2013 were $1.09 million, an increase of more than 17% compared to the same period in 2012.

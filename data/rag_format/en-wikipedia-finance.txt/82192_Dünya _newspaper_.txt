# Dünya (newspaper)



***Dünya*** (Turkish: *The World*) is a Turkish newspaper founded in 1981 by Nezih Demirkent [tr], who was also its editor-in-chief in his lifetime. The newspaper covers mainly business news and has a circulation of around 55,000.

# List of banks in Sweden



This is a **list of banks in Sweden**, updated from official Swedish financial regulator Finansinspektionen on 2008-03-11.

At the end of 2023, there were 123 banks in Sweden. They can be divided into four groups: Swedish corporate banks, foreign banks, savings banks and member banks. Sweden's largest banks regardless of measurement type (employees, revenue, capitalisation, assets) is typically a list of four – Swedbank, Nordea, SEB and Handelsbanken.

Swedish banks and financial institutions dominate the financial market in the Baltic states – Lithuania, Latvia and Estonia.

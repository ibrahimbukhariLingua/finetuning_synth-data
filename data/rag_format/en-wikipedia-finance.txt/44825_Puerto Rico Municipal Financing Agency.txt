The **Puerto Rico Municipal Financing Agency** (**MFA**) —Spanish: *Agencia para el Financiamiento Municipal de Puerto Rico* (**AFM**)— is the municipal bond issuer and an intragovernmental bank of the government of Puerto Rico. The Agency is a government-owned corporation of the government of Puerto Rico and a principal affiliate of the Puerto Rico Government Development Bank.


## Bonds issued

* Series A Bonds
* Series B Refunding Bonds
* Series C Refunding Bonds

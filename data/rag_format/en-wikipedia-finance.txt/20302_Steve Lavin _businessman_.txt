# Steve Lavin (businessman)



**Steven H. Lavin** (born 1955) is an American billionaire businessman, and the chairman of OSI Group, a US meat processor and the main hamburger supplier to McDonald's. As of August 2024, *Forbes* estimated his net worth at US$3.9 billion.


## Early life

He is the son of Sheldon Lavin, who was the owner, chief executive officer (CEO) and chairman of OSI Group. Lavin earned a BS in accountancy from the University of Illinois in 1977, and a JD from Loyola University Chicago School of Law in 1980.


## Career

Lavin trained as a corporate attorney, and is president of Lavin & Gedville in Chicago. He is also chairman of Bank Leumi. In 2023, he became chairman of OSI Group after his father, Sheldon Lavin.

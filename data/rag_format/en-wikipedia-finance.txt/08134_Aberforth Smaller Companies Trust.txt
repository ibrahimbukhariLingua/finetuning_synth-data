# Aberforth Smaller Companies Trust



**Aberforth Smaller Companies Trust plc** is a large British investment trust dedicated to investments in smaller companies.


## Overview

Established 10 December 1990, the company is a constituent of the FTSE 250 Index, an index of the larger companies on the London Stock Exchange.

The chairman is Richard Davidson. The company is managed by a team from Aberforth Unit Trust Managers who specialise in smaller companies funds.

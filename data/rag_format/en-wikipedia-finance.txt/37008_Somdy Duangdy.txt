# Somdy Duangdy



**Somdy Duangdy** (Lao: ສົມດີ ດວງດີ; born 1952) is a Laotian economist and politician. He has been Minister of Finance of Laos from 2007 to 2011, and again from 2016. He was born in Savannakhet Province in 1952. He was Minister of Planning and Investment from 2012 to 2016.


## Awards and honors

* Japan:
 + Grand Cordon of the Order of the Rising Sun (2019)

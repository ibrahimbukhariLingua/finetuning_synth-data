# Boomerang Bet



**Boomerang Bet** is a global online gambling company, founded in August 2023.


## History

Boomerang Bet is a global online gambling brand, launched in August 2023. Boomerang Bet is headquartered in Limassol, Cyprus. Its product offering includes several professional sports events annually for sport betting, live casino, and slots. Boomerang Bet offers personalized bonuses, and 24/7 multilingual support. The product operates globally and is available in 20+ countries.

In July 2024, Boomerang Bet became the official regional betting partner of AC Milan in Europe. This partnership represents a significant milestone for both AC Milan and Boomerang.

In August 2024, women football star player and influencer Alisha Lehmann from Juventus and the Swiss national team became a brand ambassador for Boomerang Bet.

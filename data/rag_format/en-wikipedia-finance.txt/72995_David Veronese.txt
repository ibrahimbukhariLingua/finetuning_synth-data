# David Veronese



**David Veronese** (17th century) was an Italian economist from Genoa.


## Life

Veronese's works were considered milestones for the merchant business.


## Works

* Veronese, David (1627). Prattica d'aritmetica mercantile (in Italian). In Genoua: Giuseppe Pavoni.
* Veronese, David (1644). Aritmetica prattica per principianti (in Italian). In Genoua: Giovanni Maria Farroni.

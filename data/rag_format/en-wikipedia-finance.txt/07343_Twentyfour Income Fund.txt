# Twentyfour Income Fund



**Twentyfour Income Fund** is a British investment trust. Established in 2013, it is dedicated to investing in less liquid, higher yield investments. The chairman is Trevor Ash. It is listed on the London Stock Exchange and it is a constituent of the FTSE 250 Index. It is managed by Twentyfour, which is part of the Vontobel Group.

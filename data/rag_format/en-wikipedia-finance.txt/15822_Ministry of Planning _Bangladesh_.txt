# Ministry of Planning (Bangladesh)



The **Ministry of Planning** (Bengali: পরিকল্পনা মন্ত্রণালয়; *Parikalpanā mantraṇālaẏa*) oversees the financial policies of the Bangladeshi Government, responsible for socioeconomic planning and statistics management.


## Directorates

1. Planning Division

* Bangladesh Institute of Development Studies
* Planning Commission
* National Academy for Planning and Development

2. Statistics and Informatics Division

* Bangladesh Bureau of Statistics

3. Implementation Monitoring and Evaluation Division

* Central Procurement Technical Unit

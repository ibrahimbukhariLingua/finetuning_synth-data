# Michel Pébereau



**Michel Pébereau** (born 23 January 1942) is a French businessman. He is the chairman of Banque Nationale de Paris (BNP) and its former CEO. He graduated from the École Polytechnique in 1965 and the École nationale d'administration in 1967.


## Biography

Pébereau began his business career in 1967 at the Inspection générale des finances. Three years later he joined the French Treasury and held a variety of high-ranking positions. He is a member of the board of directors for various organizations internationally.

# Puerto Rico Trade and Export Company



The **Puerto Rico Trade and Export Company** —Spanish: *Compañía de Comercio y Exportación de Puerto Rico* (**CCE**)— is the government-owned corporation of Puerto Rico that establishes the island's public policy for the development of its trade industry.

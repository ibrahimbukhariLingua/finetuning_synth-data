# Stuart Fuller



**Stuart Raymond Fuller** is an Australian lawyer. He is the National Leader, KPMG Law, at KPMG Australia. Previously, he was a partner in the banking & finance team at law firm King & Wood Mallesons.

From 1990 until 1997 he was a solicitor with Mallesons Stephen Jaques in Sydney, when he was appointed partner. In 2006 he was appointed managing partner and in 2012 he was named Global Managing Partner of the merged firm King & Wood Mallesons, a position he held until the end of 2016 before returning to practice.

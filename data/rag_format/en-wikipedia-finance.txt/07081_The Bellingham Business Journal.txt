# The Bellingham Business Journal



***The Bellingham Business Journal*** was a monthly business publication based in Bellingham, Washington, from 1992 to 2020.


## History

*The Bellingham Business Journal* was founded by Al Raines in November 1992. Robin Yeager sold the paper to *Wenatchee Business Journal* owners Mike Cassidy and Jim Corcoran in 1994. The partners sold the business to Sun News Inc. in 2001. Sound Publishing purchased the paper in August 2007 and discontinued publishing the *Bellingham Business Journa*l in March 2020, citing the COVID-19 pandemic.

# Lee Sush-der



**Lee Sush-der** (Chinese: 李述德; pinyin: *Li Shùdé*; born 29 November 1951) is a Taiwanese politician. He is currently the president of the Taiwan Stock Exchange. He was the Minister of Finance from 2008 to 2012. Lee has also served in the Taipei and Kaohsiung city governments.


## Education

Lee obtained his master's degree in business administration from Minnesota State University, Mankato.

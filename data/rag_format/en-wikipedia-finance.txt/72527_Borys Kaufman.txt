# Borys Kaufman



**Borys Rafailovych Kaufman** (Ukrainian: Борис Рафаїлович Кауфман; born 25 November 1973), is a Ukrainian businessperson, shareholder at Vertex United.


## Odessa Airport Ltd.

In 2011, ownership of 75% of Odesa International Airport was transferred to Odesa Airport Development Limited, controlled by Borys Kaufman and Oleksandr Hranovskyi. The businessmen are reconstructing the airport.

On 7 September 2023 the High Anti-Corruption Court of Ukraine arrested Kaufman with a bail of 268 million Ukrainian hryvnia a criminal case regarding the alleged illegal seizure of Odesa International Airport.

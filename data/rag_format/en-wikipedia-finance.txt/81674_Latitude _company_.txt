# Latitude (company)



**Latitude** (formerly **Venture Orbital Systems**) is a French rocket manufacturing company developing the Zephyr rocket.


## Background

The company was founded in 2020 and is located in Reims.
In June 2022 it had closed a €10 million Series A funding.
In January 2024, the company has raised $30 million for producing its rockets. In June 2024, Latitude has been awarded €15 million by the French government. It inaugurated its micro-launcher factory in Reims on 19 October 2021. A micro-launcher named Zephyr is currently under development. Its first flight is scheduled for the end of 2025.

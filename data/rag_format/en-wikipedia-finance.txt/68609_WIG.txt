# WIG



**WIG**, originally an acronym for **Warszawski Indeks Giełdowy** (Warsaw Stock Exchange Index) is the oldest index of the Warsaw Stock Exchange, introduced on the WSE's first trading session on 16 April 1991. WIG lists 330 companies (as of 8 November 2023).


## WIG subindices

* WIG20
* WIG30
* MWIG40 (before MIDWIG)
* SWIG80 (before WIRR)
* TECHWIG
* WIG-BANKI
* WIG-BUDOW
* WIG-INFO
* WIG-MEDIA
* WIG-PALIWA
* WIG-SPOŻY
* WIG-TELKO
* WIG-PL

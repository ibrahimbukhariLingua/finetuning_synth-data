# Business History (journal)



***Business History*** is a peer-reviewed academic journal covering the field of business history. It was established in 1958 by Liverpool University Press and is now published by Routledge. The editors-in-chief are Stephanie Decker (University of Birmingham and University of Gothenburg), Christina Lubinski (Copenhagen Business School), and Niall MacKenzie (University of Glasgow).

# 4iG



**4iG** is a Budapest- based IT company listed in the premium category of the Budapest Stock Exchange.
In the company's name, 4i refers to the English words International, Innovative, IT and Investment, while G refers to a group.
Its predecessor is the IT-focused FreeSoft, founded in 1990.

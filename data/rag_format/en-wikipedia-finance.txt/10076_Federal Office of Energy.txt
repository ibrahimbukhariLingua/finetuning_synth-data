# Federal Office of Energy



The **Swiss** **Federal Office of Energy (SFOE)** is a Swiss federal office responsible for issues relating to energy supply and energy consumption.


## Background

The office is attached to the Federal Department of the Environment, Transport, Energy and Communications (DETEC), and is based in Ittigen.

As of 2022, the SFOE had 324 employees, a budget of around CHF 100 million, revenues of around CHF 1.3 billion, and administered transfer credits of around CHF 1.8 billion.

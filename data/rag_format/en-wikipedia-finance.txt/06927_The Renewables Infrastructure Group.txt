# The Renewables Infrastructure Group



**The Renewables Infrastructure Group** (LSE:Â TRIG) is a large British investment trust dedicated to investments in assets generating electricity from renewable sources. Established in 2013, the company is a constituent of the FTSE 250 Index. The chairman is Richard Morse.


## Operations

As of 31 December 2021, the Renewables Infrastructure Group operates a portfolio of 83 projects with a generating capacity of 2,200MW.

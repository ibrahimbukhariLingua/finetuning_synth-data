# Industrial Bank of Korea



**Industrial Bank of Korea** (**IBK**; Korean: 중소기업은행; RR: *Jungso Gieop Eunhaeng*) is a state-owned bank headquartered in Jung-gu, Seoul, South Korea. Under the Industrial Bank of Korea Act, IBK was established to promote small and medium-sized businesses and improve their economic status by providing an efficient credit system.

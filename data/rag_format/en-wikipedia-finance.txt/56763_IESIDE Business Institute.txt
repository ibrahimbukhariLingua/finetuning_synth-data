# IESIDE Business Institute



**IESIDE Business Institute** is a private business school located in Vigo, Spain, established in 1987.


## History

Since its foundation linked to the Galician bank ABANCA, IESIDE Business School has contributed to the economic development of the euroregion Galicia-Northern Portugal.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ In 2009, it became an associated school of the University of Vigo.


## Academic programs

IESIDE Business School has multiple exchange programs for teachers and students, mainly in the US, UK, and Mexico.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ


## Accreditation

It is a member of the European Foundation for Management Development (EFMD).

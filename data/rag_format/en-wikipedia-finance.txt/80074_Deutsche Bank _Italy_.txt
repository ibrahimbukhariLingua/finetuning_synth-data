# Deutsche Bank (Italy)



**Deutsche Bank S.p.A.** is an Italian bank based in Milan, Lombardy. It is a subsidiary of Deutsche Bank A.G.


## History

**Banca dell'Italia Meridionale** was found in 1917. It was acquired by Amadeo Giannini, the founder of Bank of Italy (United States) in 1922. The bank was renamed to **Banca d'America e d'Italia** (literally the *Bank of America and Italy*).

# Air India Limited



**Air India Limited** is an Indian aviation holding company notably consisting of Air India and Air India Express headquartered in Gurugram, India. Its ownership is divided between Tata Group (74.9%) and Singapore Airlines (25.1%). On the anniversary of the airline's founder J. R. D. Tata's birth, its headquarters is in Gurugram was inaugurated.

It was previously a central public sector undertaking under the ownership of Ministry of Civil Aviation. It was incorporated on 30 March 2007 as the **National Aviation Company of India Limited** to oversee the merger of Air India, Indian Airlines . It was renamed as Air India Limited on 26 October 2010.

Air India Limited was privatized through its sale to the Tata Group. On 8 October 2021 Tata Sons paid ₹180 billion (US$2.1 billion) to Government of India and Tata will also assume ₹153 billion (US$1.8 billion) of Air India Limited's debt as per the transfer deal. The remaining ₹462.62 billion (US$5.4 billion) of Air India Limited's debt was also transferred to AlAHL.

The Tata Group requested approval from the CCI to merge AirAsia India and Vistara with Air India Limited in April 2022, which was granted on 14 June 2022.

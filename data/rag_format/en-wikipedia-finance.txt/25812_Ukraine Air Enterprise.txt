# Ukraine Air Enterprise



**Ukraine Air Enterprise** (Ukrainian: Державне авіаційне підприємство «Україна», romanized: *Derzhavne aviatsiine pidpryiemstvo "Ukraina"*) is a government-owned airline based in Kyiv, Ukraine. It was established and started operations in 1996 and operates wet-lease flights on behalf of a number of local organisations including the Ukrainian government.

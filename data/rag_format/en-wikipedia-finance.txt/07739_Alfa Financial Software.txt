# Alfa Financial Software



**Alfa Financial Software Holdings plc** is a business providing software for customers working in the asset finance industry. It is listed on the London Stock Exchange and is a constituent of the FTSE 250 Index.


## History

The company was founded by Justin Cooper, Ian Hargrave and Andrew Page as CHP Consulting in 1990 and then rebranded as Alfa Financial Software in November 2016. It was the subject of an initial public offering in summer 2017.

Trading as Alfa, the company provides software to asset finance customers in 18 countries and has offices in London, Detroit, Los Angeles and Auckland.

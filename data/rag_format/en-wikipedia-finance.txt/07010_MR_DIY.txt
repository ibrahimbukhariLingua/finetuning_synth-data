# MR.DIY



**MR.DIY**, stylized as **MR.D.I.Y.**, is the largest home improvement retailer in Malaysia. Owned by Malaysian company Mr D.I.Y. Group, it offers home improvement products for do-it-yourself through its network of shops and online stores.

Mr D.I.Y. Group owns and operates over 1,000 stores in Malaysia and over 3,000 stores in 10 Asian and European markets, including Malaysia, Thailand, Indonesia, the Philippines, India, Turkey, Spain, Singapore, Brunei, Cambodia, Hong Kong, and Vietnam.


## History

MR. DIY was started as a consumer hardware store in Jalan Tuanku Abdul Rahman, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia in July 2005. In 2018, MR. DIY embraced e-commerce by establishing an online store and presence on Shopee. In October 2020, MR. DIY became a publicly traded company on the Bursa Malaysia stock exchange. MR. DIY's international expansion began in November 2021 with the opening of its first store in Turkey at Meydan Istanbul Mall. This was followed by the launch of stores in Spain (Talavera de la Reina and Madrid) in January 2022. As of October 2022, the company has presence in 24 locations across Turkey and 12 in Spain.

In April 2021, the company launched a storefront format called MR.DIY Express for rural communities. In May 2022, the group opened MR.DIY Plus, a 30,000 sq ft (2,787 m²) mega retail store, MR.DIY Plus at MidValley Megamall.

In October 2023, MR. DIY expanded its operations in Vietnam through opening five stores in three key locations within a month. Also in 2023, MR. DIY fully entered the e-commerce space of the country as it launched its official online store. In January 2024, MR. DIY unveiled its plan to operate in Romania and Bulgaria.

On 14 November 2024, the company launched a new mascot called PANDAi. The mascot, resembles the a panda, was chosen because of the universal associations, cuteness, friendliness and intelligence of the animal.

# Green Britain Group




**Green Britain Group** is a British investment company based in Stroud, Gloucestershire, England, which specialises in environmentally-friendly businesses.


## History

The company was founded in February 2020 by Dale Vince in order to encompass the entrepreneur's business enterprises which include Ecotricity, a green energy provider, Forest Green Rovers, a sustainable football club, and Ecojet, which plans to operate carbon neutral flights by 2025.

The company appointed Asif Rehmanwala as its Chief Executive in May 2023, alongside Chairman Dale Vince.

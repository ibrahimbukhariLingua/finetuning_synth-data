# Black Widow Business



***Black Widow Business*** (後妻業の女, *Gosaigyō no Onna*) is a 2016 Japanese crime comedy film directed by Yasuo Tsuruhashi [ja]. It was released in Japan by Toho on August 27, 2016.

## Reception

The film was 4th placed at the Japanese box office on its opening weekend, with 182,555 admissions, and grossed ¥215 million.

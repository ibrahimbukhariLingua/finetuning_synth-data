# Plug-in electric vehicles in Connecticut



As of July 2023ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, there were about 36,000 electric vehicles registered in Connecticut. About 25% of vehicles registered in the state between July and December 2021 were electric.


## Government policy

As of May 2022ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, the state government offers tax rebates of up to $4,250 for purchases of electric vehicles.


## Charging stations

As of March 2022ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, the state government offers tax rebates of $500 for home installations of AC level 2 charging stations.

The Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act, signed into law in November 2021, allocates US$53 million to charging stations in Connecticut.


## By region


### Bridgeport

As of December 2021ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, there were about 1,300 electric vehicles registered in Greenwich, 1,000 in Stamford, and 900 in Westport.


### New Haven

As of April 2024ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, there were 45 electric vehicles in the New Haven city fleet.

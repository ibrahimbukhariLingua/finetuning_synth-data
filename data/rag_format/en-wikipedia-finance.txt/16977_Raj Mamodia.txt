# Raj Mamodia



**Raj Mamodia** (born July 18, 1971) is an Indian-American business executive. He served as the CEO of Collabera in 2013, and founded Brillio in 2014, a technology company spun off from Collabera, where he is serving as Chairman and CEO. He has been awarded American Business Award and the Mid-Market Social Impact Award.


## Early life

Raj Mamodia was born on July 18, 1971, in Jaipur, in the Indian state of Rajasthan.

He studied mechanical engineering at Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology Allahabad and earned an MBA from Northwestern University Kellogg School of Management.


## Career

Before assuming the position of CEO at Collabera in 2013, Mamodia worked at information technology company Cognizant.

In 2014, the consulting division was separated from the parent company and incorporated as Brillio. Mamodia served as Founder, Chairman, and CEO.

He is the founder of a philanthropic organization.


## Recognition

* American Business Award (2017)
* Mid-Market Social Impact Award (2017)

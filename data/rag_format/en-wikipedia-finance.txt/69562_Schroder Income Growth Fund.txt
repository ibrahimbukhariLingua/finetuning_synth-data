# Schroder Income Growth Fund



**The Schroder Income Growth Fund plc** is an investment trust that aims to achieve income growth that exceeds inflation and capital growth as a result of that rising income.

It primarily invests in the United Kingdom with up to 20% of the portfolio being invested in equities on a stock exchange based outside of the UK such as on BATS Chi-X Europe.

The fund is currently managed by Sue Noffke, the Head of UK Equities at Schroders.


## History

Founded in 1995, the Schroder Income Growth Fund has been relatively successful in its aims, providing investors with an unbroken 27-year record of annual distribution increases. Since its creation, it has been on the London Stock Exchange with the ticker SCF.

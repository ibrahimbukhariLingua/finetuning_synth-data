# Min Bahadur Gurung



**Min Bahadur Gurung** is a Nepalese entrepreneur who is the chairman and managing director of the Bhat Bhateni Supermarket chain, which employs around 4,500 staff.


## Career

Gurung's career began with a small retail outlet, which has since expanded into a nationwide chain with multiple locations. He has created employment opportunities and provided a wide range of products to consumers across the country.

Gurung holds a master's degree in Economics. He is married to Sabitri Ghale Gurung.

In June 2023, Gurung was arrested in connection with the Lalita Niwas land grab scam.

In September 2024, he donated over NRs 110 million to the flood and landslide victim in Nepal.

# Ayeyarwady Bank



**Ayeyarwady Bank Public Company Limited** (Burmese: ဧရာဝတီဘဏ်) is an institutional bank headquartered in Yangon.


## Products

* Deposit
* Loan and Advances
* Remittances
* Card Payment
* E-Banking Services
* Cash Management
* Trade services
* Royal Banking
* Foreign Exchange Service
* Bancassurance

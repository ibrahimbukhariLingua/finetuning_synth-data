# Diana Department Store



**Diana Department Store** (Thai: ไดอาน่า ดีพาร์ทเมนท์สโตร์) is a chain of department stores in southern Thailand. It was a public company with a ticker symbol CGD and traded on the SET.


## Background

The company has three stores, one in Pattani and two in Hat Yai, Songkhla Province. It also has a chain of Di-Mart convenience stores in Songkhla and Pattani.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ

In 2006, Diana Department Store exited from the Stock Exchange of Thailand, sold under backdoor of DragonOne company. The old SET code was DIANA.

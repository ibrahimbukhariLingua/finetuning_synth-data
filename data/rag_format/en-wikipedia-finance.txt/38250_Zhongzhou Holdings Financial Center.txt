# Zhongzhou Holdings Financial Center



**Zhongzhou Holdings Financial Center** (Chinese: 中洲控股中心) is a 300.8-metre (987-foot) supertall skyscraper in Nanshan district, Shenzhen, Guangdong, China. Construction started in 2009 and was completed in 2014. The building is mixed-use and contains a 340-room Marriott hotel with a large atrium in the upper floors. The atrium is clearly visible from the outside.


## Location

It is situated within the Nanshan CBD development within walking distance from Houhai Station of Shenzhen Metro. Neighbouring buildings include the Tencent Binhai Mansion, Shenzhen Bay Sports Center, One Shenzhen Bay, China Resources Headquarters and Coastal City.

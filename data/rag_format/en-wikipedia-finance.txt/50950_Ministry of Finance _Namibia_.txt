The Namibian **Ministry of Finance** (**MOF**) was established at Namibian independence in 1990. The first Namibian minister of finance was German Namibian Otto Herrigel. The currentᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ finance minister is Iipumbu Shiimi, a former governor of the Bank of Namibia.

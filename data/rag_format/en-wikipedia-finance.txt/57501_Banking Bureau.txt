# Banking Bureau



The **Banking Bureau** (traditional Chinese: 銀行局; simplified Chinese: 银行局; pinyin: *Yínháng Jú*) is a bureau of the Financial Supervisory Commission of Taiwan.


## Organizational structures

* Legal Affairs
* Domestic Banks
* Credit Cooperatives
* Trust and Bills Finance Co.
* Foreign Banks
* Financial Holdings Co.
* IT Office
* Accounting Office
* Secretary Office
* Statistics Office
* Personnel Office
* Ethics Office

# TEPPCO Partners



Texas Eastern Products Pipeline Company, **TEPPCO Partners LP** was a Fortune 300 company based in Houston, Texas. The company operated petroleum pipelines. It was owned by Sullivan Trillian Partners of San Antonio.


## History

During the second quarter of 2007, it was acquired by another Fortune 500 company, Houston-based Enterprise GP Holdings (NYSE: EPE) along with 35 percent of Dallas-based Energy Transfer Equity LP (NYSE: ETE).

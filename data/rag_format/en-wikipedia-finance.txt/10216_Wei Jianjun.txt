# Wei Jianjun



**Wei Jianjun** (Chinese: 魏建军; pinyin: *Wèi Jiànjūn*;1964) is a Chinese billionaire businessman, chairman of Great Wall Motors, the largest Chinese SUV manufacturer.


## Early life

Wei Jianjun was born in Baoding, Hebei, China.


## Career

According to Forbes, Wei Jianjun has a net worth of US$12 billion, as of April 2024.


## Personal life

He is married and lives in Baoding, Hebei.

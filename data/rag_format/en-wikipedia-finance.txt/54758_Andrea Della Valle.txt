# Andrea Della Valle



**Andrea Della Valle** is an Italian businessman and billionaire. He is the vice-chairman of the Tod's Group, of which his brother Diego Della Valle is chairman.


## Career

Della Valle was the co-owner of the ACF Fiorentina (for 1% shares), serving as its chairman until 2009.

In 2004, Della Valle and his brother Diego took a 40.6 percent stake in eyewear company Marcolin through their holdings.


## Recognition

* 2022: Honorary degree in economics and management, Marche Polytechnic University
* 2024: Honorary citizen, Arquata del Tronto

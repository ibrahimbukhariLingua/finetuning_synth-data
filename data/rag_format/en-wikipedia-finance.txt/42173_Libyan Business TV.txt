# Libyan Business TV



**Libyan Business TV (Iqtisadia)** (Arabic: قناة ليبيا الاقتصادية) is a Libyan business satellite channel that is owned by Crystal Media and broadcasts mainly in the Middle East and North Africa.


## Facilities

Libyan Business TV's studios are located in Amman, Jordan.

The facility consists of one large single studio which houses a number of permanent sets used for different programs.

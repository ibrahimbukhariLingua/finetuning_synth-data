# Banque Nationale Agricole



**Banque Nationale Agricole** (BNA) is a state-controlled bank in Tunisia. It is listed in the Bourse de Tunis.


## History

The Banque Nationale Agricole was created on 1 June 1959, and launched by President Habib Bourguiba on 10 October 1959. In 1969, it changed its name to Banque Nationale de Tunisie.

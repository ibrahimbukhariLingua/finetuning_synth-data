# Larissa Posner



**Larissa Posner** is an American businesswoman. She is the co-founder and chief executive officer (CEO) of StyleScan.


## Biography

Posner studied the business application of machine learning at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.

In 2017, Posner joined Verition Fund Management, a hedge fund founded by former Amaranth Advisors' executives, as a vice president. Prior to that, she advised companies on investor relations and strategies.

While working in quantitative finance in 2018, Posner observed the closure of several physical retail stores, attributing it in part to the rise of online shopping. Based on this observation, she co-founded StyleScan, a B2B software specializing in AI, where she serves as CEO.

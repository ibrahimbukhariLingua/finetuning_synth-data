# Ministry of Economy (Kosovo)



The **Ministry of Economy** (Albanian: *Ministria e Ekonomisë*) is a department of the government of Kosovo responsible for the economic policy of Kosovo. The ministry has its headquarters in Pristina, with Artane Rizvanolli as the incumbent economic minister in the second cabinet of Albin Kurti.

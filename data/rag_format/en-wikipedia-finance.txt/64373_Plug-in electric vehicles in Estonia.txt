# Plug-in electric vehicles in Estonia



As of October 2022ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, there were about 3,300 electric vehicles in Estonia. As of 2022ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, 3% of new cars sold in Estonia were electric.


## Government policy

As of July 2022ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, the Estonian government offers subsidies of up to €5,000 for electric car purchases.


## Charging stations

As of October 2022ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ, there were about 400 public charging stations in Estonia.

Starting in 2025, all new public parking lots constructed must be equipped with infrastructure for electric car charging.


## Public opinion

In a 2022 survey conducted by EY, 52% of respondents said that they would prefer to buy an electric car for their next car purchase.

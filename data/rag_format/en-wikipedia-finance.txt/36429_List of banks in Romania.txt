# List of banks in Romania



This is a list of all the banks incorporated in Romania as of 9 December 2023ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ.

## List of systemic banks

As of 2024, the National Committee of Macroprudential Oversight of Romania has identified 9 locally-incorporated banks as systematically important and thus have to meet more stringent requirements:

* Banca Transilvania
* BRD – Groupe Société Générale
* Banca Comercială Română (Erste Group)
* UniCredit Bank
* Raiffeisen Bank
* CEC Bank
* OTP Bank
* Alpha Bank
* Exim Banca Românească

# Sharad Kohli



**Sharad Kohli** (born April 2, 1966)ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ is an Indian economist, entrepreneur, finance expert, fashion icon, and speaker. He is the founder and chairman of the KCC Group. Sharad Kohli was born and raised in Ghaziabad, India.

# Ministry of Economy (Montenegro)



**Minister of Economy** (Montenegrin: Ministar ekonomije / Министар економије) is the person in charge of the Ministry of Economy of Montenegro (Ministarstvo ekonomije). Aleksandar Damjanović is the current Minister of Economy, since 4 December 2020.

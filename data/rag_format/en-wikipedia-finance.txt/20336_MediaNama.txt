# MediaNama



**MediaNama** is an Indian digital media platform that provides news, analysis, and commentary on issues related to the digital economy, technology, business, and policy. It was founded by Nikhil Pahwa in 2008.


## History

MediaNama was founded in 2008 by Nikhil Pahwa, an Indian journalist and digital media expert. Initially focused on covering the rapidly growing technology sector in India, the platform expanded to include discussions on regulatory policies, the digital economy, and the role of emerging technologies in shaping the future of business in India.

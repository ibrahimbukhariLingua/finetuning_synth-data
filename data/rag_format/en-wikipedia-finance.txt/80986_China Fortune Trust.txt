# China Fortune Trust



**China Fortune International Trust Co., Ltd.** known as **China Fortune Trust** is a Chinese investment management company which license to create private equity fund (as trust). The company is a wholly owned subsidiary of state-owned China Huadian Corporation.


## History

China Fortune International Trust Co., Ltd. was re-registered on 24 December 2008, after Huadian acquired the license from dormant company Foshan International Trust Investment Co., Ltd.. The company was also relocated from Foshan to Beijing.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ


## Private equity funds

* Xinbao N°47 Trust: 99.67% stake of Taishan Huacheng Technology (a shareholder of Puhua Investment and Taian Bank) as pledge, CN¥1.371 billion borrowed to Jiexintai.

# Bi1



**Bi1** is a hypermarket chain in Poland and supermarket chain in France. Currently, eight large-format stores operate under this banner, including seven locations acquired from Real and a hypermarket in Ełk, established specifically for the chain and opened on March 3, 2021. These stores are managed by Rella Investments.

Additionally, the Schiever Group owns two centers in Poland featuring Auchan hypermarkets (located in Racibórz and Zielona Góra), which are overseen by Schiever Polska.

The Bi1 brand also exists in its native French market, where it encompasses a chain of supermarkets with smaller retail spaces.


## Poland

In Poland bi1 was created due to acquisition of Real by Auchan.

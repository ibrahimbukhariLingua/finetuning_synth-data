# Shanghai Financial Post



The ***Shanghai Financial Post*** (Chinese: 上海金融报), or *Shanghai jinrong bao*, commonly known as ***Shanghai Financial News***, was a Shanghai-based Chinese-language financial newspaper published in China. The newspaper was sponsored by the Shanghai Branch of the People's Bank of China (中国人民银行上海分行) on July 1, 1992.


## History

*Shanghai Financial Post* was founded July 1, 1992. It began publishing color newspapers in 2000. On January 1, 2020, the newspaper ceased publication.

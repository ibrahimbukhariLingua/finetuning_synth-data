# Tower Financial Center



The **Tower Financial Center** (also known as the **TowerBank Financial Center**) is an office skyscraper in Panama City, Panama. Built between 2008 and 2011, the tower stands at 231 m (758 ft) tall with 52 floors and is currently the 13th tallest building in Panama City.


## History


### Architecture

The tower is located in the Campo Alegre neighbourhood of Panama City and it hosts three types of offices: one per floor with a total area of 1,124 m² (12,100 sq ft), two per floor with 562 m² (6,050 sq ft), and four per floor with 202 or 360 m² (3,880 sq ft). Upon the tenancy, the office spaces are delivered with finishes with modular space.

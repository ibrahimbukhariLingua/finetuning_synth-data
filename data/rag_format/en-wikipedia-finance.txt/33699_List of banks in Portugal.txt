# List of banks in Portugal



This is a list of all the banks operating in Portugal as of 8 February 2024ᵘᵖᵈᵃᵗᵉ and some defunct banks.


## Central bank

* Banco de Portugal

## Systemic Portuguese banks

These banks are domestic systemically important bank and are supervised by European Banking Supervision (the supervisory arm of the ECB).

* Banco Comercial Português
* Caixa Geral de Depósitos
* Novo Banco

## Portuguese branches of international banks

* Abanca
* Banco BAI Europa (Portuguese subsidiary of Banco Angolano de Investimentos - BAI)
* Banco Português de Investimento (owned by CaixaBank)
* Banco Santander Totta

## Other major banks

* Banco BIG
* Banco Carregosa [pt]
* Banco CTT
* Banco de Negócios Internacional
* Banco do Brasil
* Banco Efisa
* Banco Finantia [pt]
* Banco Invest
* Banco Português de Gestão
* Banco Português de Investimento
* Crédito Agrícola [pt]
* EuroBic [pt]
* Montepio Geral

## Defunct banks

* Banco Espírito Santo : bailed-out by the Portuguese state in 2014 and re-organised into Novobanco
* Banco Português do Atlântico
* Banco Português de Negócios : sold to the Angolese Bank Banco BIC in 2012
* Banco Privado Português
* Banif Financial Group : bailed-out by the Portuguese state in 2015 and sold to Banco Santander

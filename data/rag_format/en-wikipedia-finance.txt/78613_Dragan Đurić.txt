# Dragan Đurić



**Dragan Đurić** (Serbian Cyrillic: Драган Ђурић; born 20 January 1954) is a Serbian businessman, economist and former president of FK Partizan football club. He is also a president and owner of ZEKSTRA Group.


## FK Partizan

On 23 July 2008 he was selected as president of FK Partizan. His predecessor was Tomislav Karadžić.

# Jiffy (convenience store)



**Jiffy** is a Thai chain of convenience stores. There are a total of 149 Jiffy convenience stores, mostly in petrol stations.


## Companies

* Jiffy Convenience Store
* Jiffy Express
* Jiffy Kitchen
* Jiffy Bistro
* Jiffy Market
* Jiffy Daily
* Jiffy Super Fresh Market
* Jiffy Plus Supermarket

## Revenue

Jiffy stores generate monthly revenue of 2.2 million baht each, while 7-Elevens generate 1.2 million baht each, due to their differing sizes.ᶜⁱᵗᵃᵗⁱᵒⁿ ⁿᵉᵉᵈᵉᵈ

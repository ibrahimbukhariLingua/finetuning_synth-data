# 2023–24 Sindh budget



The **2023–24 Sindh budget** was presented by Syed Murad Ali Shah, the chief minister and the minister of finance of Sindh, on 11 June 2023 in front of the Sindh Assembly.


## Details

The budget's outlay for the upcoming fiscal year 2023–2024 is Rs2247.581 billion.

The budget is focused on helping those harmed by the floods and giving the province poor social protection. To reach Rs700 billion, the development budget has grown by 72%. The development budget does not, however, include any funding for environmental protection.

A number of important initiatives are included in the budget that are intended to improve education quality and increase access to learning opportunities.

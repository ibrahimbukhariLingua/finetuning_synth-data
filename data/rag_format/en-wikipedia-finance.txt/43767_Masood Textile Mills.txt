# Masood Textile Mills



**Masood Textile Mills (MTM)** (Urdu: مسعود ٹیکسٹائل ملز) is a Pakistani textile manufacturing company based in Faisalabad. It has yarn, yarn, knitting, fabric dyeing, processing, laundry and apparel manufacturing facilities. It is publicly listed on the Pakistan Stock Exchange.


## History

Masood Textile Mills was founded in 1984. In 2014 it posted a pre-tax profit of Rs 1.1 billion, which was 13% higher than its pre-tax earnings in the preceding year. Shanghai Challenge Textile Company acquired 24.3% shares in Masood Textile Mills in a privately negotiated deal. It later acquired 52% stake of the company. It used to be the largest knit-wear manufacturer and exporter in Pakistan before 2020.

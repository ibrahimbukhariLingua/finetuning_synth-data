# Boyer's Food Markets, Inc.



**Boyer's Food Markets, Inc.** is an American supermarket chain that operates stores in eastern Pennsylvania.


## History

Boyer's was founded in 1949 by Harold S. Boyer in Orwigsburg, Pennsylvania. The company has since expanded to a total of 19 stores in eastern Pennsylvania, including Ashland, Bernville, Berwick, Birdsboro, Elysburg, Fleetwood, Frackville, Hazleton, Lansford, Lykens, McAdoo, Mt. Carmel, Orwigsburg, Schuylkill Haven, Shenandoah, Tamaqua, Womelsdorf, and Yorkville and Pine Grove.

# Polish Economy (film)



***Polish Management*** or ***Polish Economy*** (German: ***Polnische Wirtschaft***) is a 1928 German propaganda silent film directed by E. W. Emo and starring Iwa Wanja, Margot Landa, and Hans Brausewetter. It was one of a number of films attacking Poland's ownership of lands in which ethnic Germans lived during the 1920s.

The film's art direction was by Kurt Richter.

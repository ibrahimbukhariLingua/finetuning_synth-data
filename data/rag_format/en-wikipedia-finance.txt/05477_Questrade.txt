# Questrade



**Questrade** is a Canadian online brokerage firm and wealth management firm. It is Canada's largest discount broker.


## Products and services

The company was created by Edward Kholodenko and launched in 1999. As of early 2020, the company was Canada's fastest growing online brokerage firm, and has $30 billion under management, as of December 2024. Questrade has expanded to include robo-advising with its Questwealth Portfolios, which invests in portfolios based on ETFs. In December 2019, Questrade applied for a banking license signalling its intent to offer banking services.

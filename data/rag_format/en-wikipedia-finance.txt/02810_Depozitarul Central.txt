# Depozitarul Central



**Depozitarul Central** (lit. 'central depository') is the main central securities depository of Romania, headquartered in Bucharest. As of late 2021, the Bucharest Stock Exchange (BVB) held 69.04 percent of Depozitarul Central's equity capital.⁵⁹


## Overview

On 11 May 2006, the BVB acquired control of Regisco,³⁸ which it renamed as Depozitarul Central on 3 January 2007.

In June 2015, Depozitarul Central connected to TARGET2-Securities (T2S), the Eurosystem's securities settlement platform, as part of the first wave of CSDs connecting to T2S.

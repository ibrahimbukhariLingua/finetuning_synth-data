# Lac à Paul mine



**Arianne Phosphate** is a mining company located in Quebec developing its **Lac à Paul project**, one of the largest phosphates reserve in Canada. having estimated reserves of 590 million tonnes of ore grading 7.13% P₂O₅. Arianne Phosphate Inc. is listed on the TSX Venture Exchange under the symbol "DAN".


## Location

The Lac à Paul project is located north of the 49th parallel (an area covered by the Plan Nord), north of Saguenay-Lac-Saint-Jean region.

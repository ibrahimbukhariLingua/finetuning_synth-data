# Shadow Exchequer Secretary to the Treasury



The **shadow exchequer secretary to the treasury** is a member of the Official Opposition frontbench in the United Kingdom. The officeholder shadows the exchequer secretary to the treasury. The current officeholder is Conservative MP Gareth Davies shadowing James Murray on the Treasury bench.

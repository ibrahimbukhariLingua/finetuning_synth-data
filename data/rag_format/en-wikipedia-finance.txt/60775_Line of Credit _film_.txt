# Line of Credit (film)



 ***Line of Credit*** (Georgian: კრედიტის ლიმიტი) is a 2014 made in Georgia, it is a comedy-drama film written and directed by Salomé Alexi. It was screened in the Horizons section at the 71st Venice International Film Festival.
